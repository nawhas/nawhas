mere ashko ki zamanay zara keemat kerna

gham-e-Shabbir main rona meri sunnat kerna

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x3

  1. ummay salwa say riwaayat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x2

  2. apnay mehoob Muhammad ko haan dekha ik din

Ghar main bethay thay ke aye wahan HUSSAIN o HASSAN

godi lay ker unhain betaab Nabi hone lage

baad Hamza kay pehli baar Nabi ronay lagay

apnay Qasim kay liye bhi na aisa roya tha

dono shezaado(n) ki khatir woh jaisa roya tha

main Mohammad ki jo haalat hay bayaa(n) kerta hoon

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x2

  3. Mene poocha meri jaan kiya huwa Mehboob-e-Khuda

Mujh say fermaya ke na pooch ay Umm-e-Salma

Mene phir arz ki ay jaa(n) meri Allah ke Nabi

aisi halat ne mene aap ki dekhi na kabhi

Kiske ghum may yeh khoolay gaisoo hain aur ashq rawaa

haye kiya baat hay kay sar per ammama na raha

ani aaj kiya qiyaamut hay bayaa(n) kerta hoon

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x2

  4. Mujh say farmaane lage dekho ay Umm-e-Salma

Yeh jo Matti hay isay kehtay hain sab karbobala

haye ik roz wahan mera nawasa hoga

Jaam-e-kauser ka yeh maliq wahan pyaasa hoga

surkh ho jaye yeh matti to samjhna bibi

meray Shabbir ki be juram Shahadut hogi

Umm-e-Salma ye haqeeqat hay bayaa(n) kerta hoon

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x2

  5. ho ga tanha usse phir aakay laiyee ghayrenge

us kay sukhay galay pay phir woh churi pherenge

us ke sar kaat ke phir naizay pay uthaayenge

mujhe Haider haye Zahra ko woh rulaayenge

us ka sar naizay say neechay layee(n) utarenga

ik najis hontoo pay us ko chari marega

haye kaisi woh aziyyat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x2

  6. Umm-e-salma meray bachay ka sada gham kerna

meri sunnat hay meray bachay ka matam kerna

kis kay mernay say koi pehlay nahi rota hay

mujhe Shabbir pay rone ka hukam hota hai

meray ashko(n) ki zamanay zara keemat karna

Gham-e-Shabbir pay rona meri sunnat kerna

Yeh Muhammad ki wasiyyat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon

  7. Umm-e-Salma yeh riwayyat teri sar aankho(n) par

Gham-e-Shabbir say sajay hain mominoo kay ghar

yeh sachi baat zamanay ko ham sunayenge

ta qiayamt yeh wasiyyat na hum bhulaayenge

aaj "Ramzaan-o-Raza" perhtay hain jin ke Nohay

kal ko aulaad hamari perhay in kay Nohay

is say zyaada kya ghanimat hay Bayaa(n) kerta hoon

(Rona shabbir pay sunnat hai bayaa(n) kerta hoon) x2
