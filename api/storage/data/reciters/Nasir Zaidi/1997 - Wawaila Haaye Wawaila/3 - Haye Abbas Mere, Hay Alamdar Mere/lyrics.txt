Note For Reciters : This Nauha Contains "Doray". Doray (Tehtay Lafz) Are Words
Not Recited In A Tone, During The Course Of Recitation. Doray Are Usually
Recited During Processions Or Where There Is A High Response (Matam) From The
People. It Would Be Advisable To Avoid During Normal Mehfil Recitations. These
Are Marked With "------".

Jukta Huwa Alam Jo Nazar Aya Shah Ko. Wapas Jo Dekha Nehr Pa Ate Sipah Ko.
Bole Hussain Kaise Ke Aik Lambhi Aah Ko. Benur Kardiya Mere Bhayi Nigaah Ko.

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  1. (Chalgayi Bazu Tere) X2 Kalb Pe Talwar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  2. Bund Pani Ke Liye Haat Katane Wale. Pyaase Bacho Ke Liye Khun Bahane Wale. (Kisliye Sogaya Tu) X2 Nehr Pe Gamkhwar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  3. Tere Dam Pe Mere Lashkar Ka Bharam Baki Tha. Ay Alamdar Tere Sath Alam Baki Tha (Kardiya Tanha Muje) X2 Tune Madadgar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  4. Juk Gayi Meri Kamar Phat Gaya Sina Mera. Jis Gari Kat Ke Gira Khak Pe Bazu Tera (Senkhro Tir Kale) X2 Je Se Huwe Saar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  5. Pani Jis Dam Se Bhara Khun Bahayaa Us Wakt. Mashke Ke Sath Jigar Karliya Zakhmi Apna (Tere Kurban Gayaa) X2 Me To Wafadaar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  6. Alatash Ki Ye Sadaa Tum Bhi To Sunte Hote. Khaymo Me Aaho Bukaa Tum Bhi To Sunte Hote (Tu Fisha Gham Me Tere) X2 Hyder O Diwaar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2

  7. Khali Kuzo Me Bhare Bacho Ne Ansu Apne. Kehti Hai Bali Sakina Koyi Faryad Sune (Legaya Chin Ke Zur) X2 Shimr Sitamghaar Mere

(Haye Abbas Mere, Hay Alamdar Mere) X2
