Marte Dum Jinko Pani Pilana Na Tha

Unko Ghar Se Munasib Bulana Na Tha



1.     Kyon Na Shah Rote Hare K Se Mil Kar Gale,

Phir Palat Kar Madine Me Ana Na Tha



2.     Jisse Sajjad (A.s.) Ka Khoon Tapakta Na Ho,

Haye Aisa Koi Taziyana Na Tha



3.     Saans Bhi Le Na Sajte The Ahle Haram,

Qabr Zindon Kit H Qaidkhana Na Tha



4.     Shamiyon Kalmago Jis Payambar (S.a.) Ke Ho,

Uske Ghar Ka Munasib Jalana Na Tha



5.     Zibha Sayyad Ko Tashna Dahan Kyon Kiya,

Jurm Pyase Ko Pani Pilana Na Tha



6.     Laashe Sarwar (A.s.) Ko Sajjad (A.s.) Dete Kafan,

Is Qadar Bhi Muaafiq Zaman Na Tha



7.     Kaun Ghurbat Me Shabbir (A.s.) Ko Puchta,

Wo Rasool-E-Khuda (S.a.) Ka Zaman Na Tha



8.     Bano Chillai Asghar (A.s.) Ka Sir Dekhkar,

Tere Marne Ka Beta Zamana Na Tha



9.     Tu Lahad Se Meri Jaa Nikala Gaya,

Tere Zere Zamee Bhi Thikana Na Tha



10.                        Dar Ba Dar Koofiyon Ne Firaya Jise,

Kya Rasool-E-Khuda (S.a.) Ka Zamana Na Tha



11.                        Qais Ki Gar Shifaat Na Karte Nabi (S.a.),

Juz Jahnnam Phir Uska Thikana Na Tha.
