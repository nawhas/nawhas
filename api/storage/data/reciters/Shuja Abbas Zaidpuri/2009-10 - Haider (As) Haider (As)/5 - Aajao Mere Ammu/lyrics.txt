1.     Baba bh sir kataye maqtal me so rahe hai,

Koi nahi sahar - aa jao mere ammu



2.     Sab ansuo ko peekar, sehraab ho chuke hai,

Koi nahi pyasa - aa jao mere ammu



3.     Looti gayi ridayen, kheme bhi jal na jaye

Hai zalimon ka nargha - aa jao mere ammu



Koi nahi humara, charo taraf hain ada

Har dil hai sahma-sahma - aa jao mere ammu



Khati hoon mai tamache aur pusht par bhi durre

Deta hai shimr (l.a.) izaa - aa jao mere ammu



Hai har taraf andhera, itna bada hai sahara,

Dhundhe kaha sakina (s.a.) \- aa jao mere ammu
