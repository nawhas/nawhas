Jagah-Jagah Se Shikasta Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha

Pada Hai Dhoop Me Maula Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



1.     Jina To Chor Ke Ayi Hain Tapte Sahara Me,

Na Chor Payengi Zahara (S.a.) Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



2.     Na Tan Pa Sir Hai Na Pehchanne Ko Hai Kurta,

To Kaise Dhundhe Sakina (S.a.) Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



3.     Humare Ashk Bhi Gin Kar Bata Nahi Sakte,

Hai Kitne Zakhmo Me Dooba Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



4.     Sakina (S.a.) Aao Idhar Aao Jald Aa Jao,

Husain (A.s.) Bole Ki Bola Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



5.     Bahan Pukari Tumhi Ho Husain (A.s.) Kya Bhai,

Ajeeb Haal Me Dekha Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



6.     Sina Ki Nok Se Zakhmi Hua Sare Zainab (S.a.)

Zamee Pa Dard Se Tadpa Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



7.     Bate Thi Pahle Bhatije Ki Laash Tukdo Me,

Hai Ab Zameen Pa Bikhra Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



8.     Aseer Ho Ke Soo-E-Sham Jab Chale Abid (A.s.),

Tha Sir Kahi To Kahi Tha Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha



9.     Tawaf Karta Hai Ek She Raise Aey Fidvi,

Ho Jaise Uska Bhi Laasha Husain (A.s.) Ka Laasha
