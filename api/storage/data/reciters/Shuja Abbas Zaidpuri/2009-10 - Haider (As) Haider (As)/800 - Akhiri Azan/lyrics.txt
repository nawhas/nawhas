Bola Shabbir (A.s.) Ka Dil Tadapkar - Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



1.     Jab Suna Allaho Akbar, Kheme Me Ro Padi Maan Tadapkar,

Karti Hai Jaan Tum Par Nichawar - Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



2.     Subah Ka Waqt Aya Hai Jab Se, Tar Musalla Kiya Aansuo Ne,

Roya Laila (S.a.) Ka Dil Haye Dilbar - Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



3.     Fufiyaan, Maan Aur Bali Sakina (S.a.), Gham Se Dushwar Hai Sab Ka Jeena,

Dasht Me Aey Shabeeh-E-Payambar (A.s.) \- Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



4.     Maan Ke Dil Me Hai Kab Se Tamanna, Haye Afsos Sehra Na Dekha,

Phool, Kaliyaan Hui Haye Paththar - Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



5.     Janibe Jang Jab Ho Rawana, Mujhko Mud-Mud K Tum Dekhe Jana,

Ro Ke Kahte The Akbar (A.s.) Se Sarwar (A.s.) \- Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



6.     Jayengi Ye Sadaye Watan Tak, Ud Ke Pahochengi Choti Bahan Tak,

Kaise Janegi Beemar Khahar - Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari



7.     Aey Shuja Ab Dua Shah Se Mango, Karbala Jald Mujhko Bula Do,

Mai Padhu Nauha Rauze Me Akar - Akhiri Hai Azaa Tumhari.
