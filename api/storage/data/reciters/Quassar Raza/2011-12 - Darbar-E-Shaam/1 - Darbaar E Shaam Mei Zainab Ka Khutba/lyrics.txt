Noha



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main

Rab Ka Kalam Shamil Hai Mare Kalam Main



1*Baghe Nye Hain Shamiyon Hum Hain Nabi Ki Aal

Qaidi Bani Hun Aj Main Gurbat Ka Hai Ya Haal

Haq Ka Payaam Deti Hun Har Khas O Aam Main

Rab Ka Kalam Shamil Hai Mare Kalam Main



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main



2*Shahzadi Ma Najaf Ki Hun Main Be Hun Sayada

Baba Ki Tarha Bolo Gye Ab Main Be Ba Khuda

Haidar Ka Lehja Hoga Ab Mare Payaam Main

Rab Ka Kalam Shamil Hai Mare Kalam Main



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main



3*Karbal Main Deen Bachaya Hai Sarwar Ney Jis Tarha

Jab Tak Nabi Ki Aal Hai Koi Be Kis Tarha

Badlao Le Ke Aye Ga Deen Ke Nizaam Main

Rab Ka Kalam Shamil Hai Mare Kalam Main



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main



4*Zainab Ne Yai Yazeed Ko Paigaam Sunaya

Hona Tha Us Ka Jo Be Wo Anjaam Bataya

Lanat Be Farz Ho Gaye Ab Tare Naam Main

Rab Ka Kalam Shamil Hai Mare Kalam Main



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main



5*Ho Jaye Tan Se Sar Judha Bazo Be Ho Qaalam

Haq Ka Payaam De Gye Ho Jitney Ab Sitam

Allah Ki Raza Be Hai Haq Ke Kalam Main

Rab Ka Kalam Shamil Hai Mare Kalam Main



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main



6*Qaussar Ki Labo Pe Hai Bus Yai Hi Sadaye

Irfan Rab Se Karta Hai Har Pal Ya Duaye

Karbobala Dekha Zara Hum Ko Inaam Main



Zainab Ka Ya Khutba Hai Darbaar E Shaam Main
