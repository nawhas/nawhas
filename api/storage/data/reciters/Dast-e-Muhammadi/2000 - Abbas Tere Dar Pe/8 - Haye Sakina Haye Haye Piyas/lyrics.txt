Haye Sakina Haye Haye Piyas_

(Haye Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X3

Kanon Say Sitamgar Merey Laigaey Gohar, Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay
Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  1. Kyu Mujh Say Khafa Hogaey Bhai Merey Saarey, Ammou Bhi Gaey Chhor Kay Or Tum Bhi Sidhary, (Baba Ye Batao Main Jioon Kis Kay Saharey) X2 Chaltey Hain Merey Qalb Pay Alaam Kay Khanjar

Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  2. Sar Pay Koi Saya Na Koi Dast-E-Karam Hay, Awaz Ye Mojon Ki Merey Dil Pay Raqam Hay (Pani Ka Naheen Mujh Ko Chacha Jan Ka Gham Hay) X2 Or Is Pay Sitam Aap Ki Furqat Hay Muqaddar

Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  3. Jo Dil Pay Guzarti Hay Woh Bas Mujh Ko Pata Hay, Har Lamha Nya Gham Hay Nai Aik Jafa Hay (Kuch Or Naheen Sirf Yateemi Ki Saza Hay) X2 Dhondoon Main Kahan Aap Ka Woh Laasha-E-Be Sar

Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  4. Aada Nay Jala Daley Hain Khaimey Bhi Hamarey, Kanon Say Gohar Khench Kay Zalim Nay Utarey (Rukhsar Pay Be Jurm Tamanchey Kai Marey) X2 Ye Neel Tamanchon Kay Zara Dekh Lo Aakar


Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  5. Karta Hay Safar Gham Kay Samandar Main Safeena, Neend Aaey Bhal Kaisey Naheen Aap Ka Seena (Tum Jab Say Gaey Soi Naheen Bali Sakina) X2 Ek Khof Hay Dil Main Ke Na Aajaey Sitamgar

Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  6. Bhari Hay Bohat Baba Merey Dil Pay Ye Sadma, Bin Aap Ke Jeeney Ka Tasawwar Hi Naheen Tha (Phat Jaaey Na Is Dard-E-Judai Say Kaleja) X2 Jalti Hui Reti Pay Tarapti Houn Khuley Sar

Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  7. Baba Ye Yateemi Bhi Ajab Zarb Hay Kaari, Daman Mai Lagi Aag Lahoo Kanaon Say Jari (Lay Aalam-E-Ghurbat Main Khabar Kon Hamari) X2 Har Sans Karey Noha Kahey Qalb Tarap Kar


Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2

  8. Kia Kia Main Likhoun Kaisey Likhoun Dard Ka Manzar, Tahreer Larazti Hay Qalam Rota Hay Safdar (Alfaz Naheen Dard O Masaib Kay Hain Khanjar) X2 Karti Thi Buka Ban Main Yahi Shah Ki Dukhtar

Ghata Ghamon Ki Chhai Baba Sitam Hay Ye Judai Baba Suno Meri Duhai Baba (Haye
Sakina Haye Haye Piyas) X2
