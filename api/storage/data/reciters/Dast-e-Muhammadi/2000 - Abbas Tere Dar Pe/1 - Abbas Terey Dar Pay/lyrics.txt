(Ay Shahensha-E-Wafa) X4

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X3

  1. (Gar Hogi Raza Teri) X2 Jaen Gay Shafa Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2

  2. Too Bab-E-Hawaij Hay Haider Ka Pisar Too Hay, Zehra Ki Duaaon Ka Aey Ghazi Asar Too Hay, Saqqaey Haram Aey Ghazi Kar Nazr-E-Karam Aey Ghazi (Jatey Hain Teray Dar Say) X2 Har Cheez Gada Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2

  3. Kis Dar Pay Bhala Jaaen Ham Chor Kay Dar Tera, Panja Jo Alam Pay Hay, Hay Dast-E-Karam Tera, Parcham Say Hawa Jo Aaey Khushboo-E-Wafa Ajaaey (Jeetey Hain Sabhi Teray) X2 Parcham Ki Hawa Lekar
  4. Milta Hi Naheen Sani Dunya Main Kaheen Tera, Na Kehna Merey Ghazi Dastoor Naheen Tera, Sakhion Kay Sakhi Aey Ghazi Har Cheez Mili Hay Ghazi (Aghyar Kay Dar Par Ham) X2 Kyu Jaaey Sada Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2

  5. Jis Ghar Main Alam Jaaey Us Ghar Say Bala Taley, Woh Nam Hay Bas Tera Jo Nam-E-Qaza Taley, Parcham Pay Nazar Jati Hay To Maut Bhi Darjati Hay (Yeh Dunya Teray Dar Say) X2 Jati Hay Baqa Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2

  6. Sar Kat Ta Naheen Shah Ka Na Lut Ta Yeh Ghar Ghazi, Yeh Zulm Naheen Hotey Tu Hota Agar Ghazi, Shabbir Ka Too Bazoo Hay Zainab Ka Dilasa Too Hay (Jata Na Koi Zalim) X2 Zainab Ki Rida Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2

  7. No Laakh Kay Lashkar Ko Pairon Say Masal Deta, Maidan-E-Wagha Ka Tu Naqsha Hi Badal Deta, Too War Agar Kardeta Lashon Say Too Run Bhardeta (Jata To Sar-E-Maqtal) X2 Too Izn-E-Wagha Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2

  8. Itna Hay Meray Ghazi Safdar Pay Karam Tera, Hay Haath Mera Laikin Qirtas-O-Qalam Tera, Dil Main Hay Mohabbat Teri Hay Mujh Pay Inayat Teri (Jaawu Main Teray Dar Say) X2 Ab Or Bhi Kia Lekar

(Abbas Terey Dar Pay Ham Aaey Dua Lekar) X2
