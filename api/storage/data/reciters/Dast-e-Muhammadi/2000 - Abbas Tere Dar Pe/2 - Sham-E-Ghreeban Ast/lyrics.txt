  2. Aey Ramz-E-Izzat-E-Khuda Aey Raz-E-Qudrat-E-Khuda, Aey Ashnaaey Kibrya Aey Lakht-E-Qalb-E-Mustafa (Ajeeb Waqt Aagia Qadam Qadam Pay Hay Jafa) x2 Aal-E-Shuma Dar Badar-O- Aajiz-O-Hairan Ast

(Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast) x2

  3. Aen Dastan-E-Khonchukan Baya Ke Man Kam Bayan, Lahoo Lahoo Ye Dastan Karon Main Kis Tarha Bayan, Sitam Hoey Hain Is Qadar (Tarap Rahey Hain Jism-O-Jan) x2 Yak Roze Qissa-E-Waley Qissa-E-Toolan Ast

(Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast) x2

  4. Be Maya O Be Chadaram Barehna Sar E Madaram, Beghair Maqna O Rida Hay Jo Ghamon Main Mubtala, (Luti Hai Ye Kuch Is Tarha Ke Paas Kuch Bhi Na Raha) x2 Aen Waris-E-Kon O Makan Be Sar-O-Saman Ast

(Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast) x2

  5. Aen Lashaae Dareeda Tan Aen Yadgar E Panjetan, Ghareeb O Tashna Be Kafan Ruka Hay Teeron Pay Badan (Sahey Ye Kis Tarha Bahan Bujha Charagh-E-Panjetan) x2 Khoon-E-Dil E Fatima Amroz Che Arzan Ast

(Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast) x2

  6. Che Shud Muhafiz-E-Haram Ke Booda Sarnigon Alam, Kahan Hay Dharas-E-Haram Jhuka Hua Hay Kioon Alam (Taraptey Hain Ahl-E-Haram Atash Say Ghut Raha Hay Dam) x2 Aab-E-Rawan E Alqama As Khud Pareshan Ast

(Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast Sham-E-Ghareeban Ast) x2

  7. Be Yeen Guroh Be Wafa Darindagan Be Haya, Ye Lashkar E Jor O Jafa Hay Befa O Be Haya (Ye Itrat E Rasool Hay Jisey Kia Hay Be Rida) x2 Ghairat-E-Deen Nabawi Sar Bagraiban Ast
