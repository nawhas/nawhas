  2. Insaf Say Bhar Dega Dunya Aaey Ga Hata Kay Jab Parda, Zulman Ki Ghata Chhat Jaaey Gi Hoga Dunya Main Noor E Khuda, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  3. Kuch Raz Woh Aisey Kholey Ga Ye Sara Jahan Haq Boley Ga, Woh Aakar Haq Or Batil Ko Meezan-E-Khuda Main Toley Ga, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  4. Karbal Kay Shaheedon Ka Badla Woh Mehdi-E-Aakhir Hi Lega, Tum Hi To Sipahi Ho Us Kay Kia Fikr Tumhain Phir Gham Kaisa, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  5. Jis Ko Hay Imamat Ka Dawa Be Maut Woh Mara Jaaey Ga, Mil Jaaey Gi Shahi Mitti Main Ek Roz Uthey Ga Jab Parda, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  6. Phir Hogi Hukoomat Maula Ki Ye Dor Bhi Dunya Dekhey Gi, Abbas Ka Parcham Qomi Nishan Or Qomi Tarana Nad-E-Ali, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  7. Eisa Ka Sukoon Mil Jaey Ga Jab Mehdi-E-Aakhir Aaey Ga, Hatif Ki Sada Tab Goonjey Gi Ye Sara Jahan Duhraaey Ga, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  8. Rank Apney Areezey Laaen Gey Ham Log Sila Youn Paaen Gey, Jab Hogi Ziarat Maula Ki Sab Zakhm-E-Jigar Sil Jaaen Gey, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2

(Rakh Maula Tay Aas) X4

  9. Kya Zaat Hay Woh Pehchan Usay Khaliq Nay Ye Di Hay Shaan Usay, Safdar Ye Naseehat Karta Hay Too Bhi To Waseela Man Usaey, Ab Woh Hay Aaney Wala (Jo Hay Sab Ka Rakhwala) X2
