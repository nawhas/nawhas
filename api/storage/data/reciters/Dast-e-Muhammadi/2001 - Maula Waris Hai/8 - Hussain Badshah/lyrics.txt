(Hussain badshah) x4

  2. Sadqa hay tera ye kon o makan (hay bheek teri ye aab e rawan) x2 kehti hay tujhey khaliq ka wali (quran ki ye khamosh zaban) x2 teri zat kia hay yahi masala hay, wahan zat teri jahan kibria hay


(Hussain badshah) x4

  3. Maqsad hay to hi har aabid ka (too far shaheed or shahid ka) x2 ye bat samajhtey hain momin (too raz hay zat e wahid ka) x2 teri martabat main teri manzilat main, likhon hamd-e-bari teri manqabat main

(Hussain badshah) x4

  4. Mehboob e khuda hay zat teri (taaley na kabhi jo bat teri) x2 tasbeeh parhain ye ahl e falak (har subha teri har raat teri) x2 teri bheek lekar ye sooraj pukarey, gawahi main uski samandar sada day


(Hussain badshah) x4

  5. Mairaj e zameer e insan hay (too hi to kalam e yazdan hay) x2 too qadr o qaza ka malik hay (is par ye zamana hairan hay) x2 tera qarz deen par khuda ki zameen par, kalam khuda ki likha hay jabeen par

(Hussain badshah) x4

  6. Jo ishq-e-faqeeri sikhladey (jo ishq-e-ameeri thukradey) x2 darwesh banadey insan ko (khaliq ki raza bhi dilwadey) x2 khuda say nabi say miloon jab kisi say, tujhay yad karkey kahoon main khushi say


(Hussain badshah) x4

  7. Kaisey ho raqam uftad teri (hay teri tarha aulad teri) x2 leta na khuda ka naam koi (milti na agar imdad teri) x2 nabi kay nawasy rahey tifl piasar, kaha alqama nay tarap kar khuda say

(Hussain badshah) x4

  8. Teeron pay badan tha kuch aisey (quran rehal pay ho jaisay) x2 ye soch kay main kaanp uthta houn (kay too nay kia sajda kaisey) x2 shariat pukari mashiat pukari, nabooat pukari ibadat pukari


(Hussain badshah) x4
