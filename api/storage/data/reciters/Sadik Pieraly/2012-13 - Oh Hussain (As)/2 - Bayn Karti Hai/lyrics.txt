1)    Kaissi Gham Ki Gatha Akey Chayi, Hogayi Sab Sey Ban Mei Judayi

2)    Aap Aey Na Ammu Bhi Aey, Khaimey Adaa Ney Akey Jalaey

Jal Gaya Mere Bhayi Ka Jhula, Waa Hussaina Hussaina Hussaina



3)    Paas Hotey Jo Qassim-O-Akbar, Sar Sey Chinti Na Bibi Chadar

Zulm Koyi Bhi Ham Pei Na Khata, Waa Hussaina Hussaina Hussaina



4)    Thi Bahot Tumko Tashna Dahani, Na Mila Ek Bund Bhi Pani

Kiya Sitam Tumpé Dhaey Hai Baba



5)    Hai Madiné Mei Ek Téri Dukhiya, Roté Roté Na Mar Jaey Sughra

Kyun Na Aey Mujhé Leyney Bhayya



6)    Hogaya Hai Yeh Ran Mei Andhéra, Ab Kahan Jaey Téri Yeh Dukhiya

Bass Sada Do Mujhé Méré Baba,



7)    Ro Raha Hai Jahan Méré Maula, Jab Sunaya Hai Sadik Ney Nauha
