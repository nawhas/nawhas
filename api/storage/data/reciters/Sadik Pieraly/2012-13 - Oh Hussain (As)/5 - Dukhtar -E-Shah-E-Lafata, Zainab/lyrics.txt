Dukhtar-E-Shah-E-Lafata, Zainab

Bey Saharon Ka Aasraa, Zainab

1)    Apney Baachon Ko Kardiya Qurbaan

Tha Yeh Tera Hi Hoslaa Zainab



2)    Kufey Aur Shaam Key Safar Mei Tamaam

Thi Mussibat Pei Wo Fidaa Zainab



3)    Zindagi Abaru Bachaney Pei

Chine Gayi Teyri Hai Ridaa Zainab



4)    Hogaey Khaak Jalkey Sab Khaimey

Ab Kahan Jaey Benawaan Zainab
