(Aagaya Bazar-E-Shaam) X4

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X3

  1. (Baloon Se Moon Ko Chhupati Hai) X2 Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  2. Ab Monis-O-Yawar Koi Nahin Imdad Kare Ab Koon Bhala

Hai Aal-E-Nabi Pe Tang Zameeen Imdad Kare Ab Koon Bhala

Is Baat Pe Ashk Bahati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  3. Kis Shaan Se Nikni Thi Gher Se Bazar Hue The Band Sabhi

Athara Barader Ka Saaya Thi Ser Pe Rida Or Mehmil Bhi

Ye Sooch Ke Dil Ko Jalati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  4. Bedard Zamane Kya Jaane Ghyrat Kya Hai Or Kya Hai Haaya

Abbas-E-Jari Ka Ser Logoon Neeze Se Zameen Per Kyun Aaya

Ghazi Se Nazar Jab Aati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  5. Oolad-E-Pyamber Per Logoon Kya Waqt Parha Sochoo To Sahi

Quran Qasidey Jin Ke Parhe Ab Naam Un Ke Leeta Hai Shaqi

Her Gaam Pe Merti Jaati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  6. Zanjeer Sambale Ik Qeedi Aankhoon Se Lahoo Bersata Hai

Kerta Hai Sawaal Ik Chadar Ka Bazar Koi Jab Aata Hai

Merjae Na Wo Ghabrati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  7. Sadmoon Se Ager Dil Bojhal Hai Larza Hai Thakan Se Qadmoon Mein

Merham Ki Jaga Kismat Ne Bhari Hai Khak-E-Safar Hi Qadmoon Mein

Is Khak Se Ser Ko Jhukati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  8. Koneen Ki Is Shehzadi Pe Tote Hain Sitam Bazaroon Mein

Amaan Ki Tarhan Ye Shehzadi Bulwai Gaye Derbaroon Mein

Her Zaqm Jigar Per Khati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2

  9. Chooma Tha Jinheen Kal Haider Ne Wo Hath Rasan Mein Jakrhe Hain

Bachi Ko Tamache Lagte Hain Bemaar Ko Durre Lagte Hain

Ghabra Ke Agar Chillati Hai Hye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader

(Bazar-E-Sitam Mein Aati Hai Haye Zainab-E-Kubra Be Chader) X2
