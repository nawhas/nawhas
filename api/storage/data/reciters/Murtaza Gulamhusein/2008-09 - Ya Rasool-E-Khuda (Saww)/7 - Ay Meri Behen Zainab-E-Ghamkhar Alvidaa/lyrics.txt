Alvida (x4)

eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)

1.     naana ke deen-e-haq ko bacchane ke waaste, hum jaa rahe hay vaada nibhane ke waaste, aayenghe hum na laut ke is baar alvidaa



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)



2.     baazoo tumhare rassi mein honge bandhe huwe, hum honge noke neza pe tum sar khule huwe, aayega jab yazid ka darbaar, alvidaa



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)



3.     jaakar watan mein kehna sabhi se mera salaam, kehna shaheed hogaya pardes mein imam, kehna ke loot gayaa mera sansaar, alvidaa



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)



4.     zindaa se aake kabro ko aabaad kijiyo, paani agar piyo to hume yaad kijiyo, khaayi hay tera bekaso laachaar alvidaa



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)



5.     baali sakina dasht mein aa jaaye naa kahin, zindaan ke andhero se ghabharaye naa kahin, rehna humare baad mein, hoshiyaar alvidaa



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)



6.     roodaad jaake amma se jisdum sunaoonga, tum har kadam pe saath rahi ho bata oonga, eh virsaa daare haider-e-karaar alvidaa



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)



7.     gulshan bharosaa kab hay kisiko hayaat ka, agle saal kaun kare zikr-e-kerbala, hay murtaza bhi, sheh ka azadaar alvidaa.



eh meri behen zainab-e-ghamkhar alvidaa (x2)
