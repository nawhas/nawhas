Aray O Shaam Walo

Ali(A.s) Ki Laadli Houn Mujhay Pather Na Maro, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)

1.     Meray Sajjad(A.s) Ko Qaidi Banaya Mai Nahi Boli

Sakina(A.s) Ka Gala Rasi Say Bandha Mai Nahi Boli

Magar Ub Bolti Houn Buss Itna Rehm Ker Du, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



2.     Mujhay Tutheer Hi Waris Sumjh Kay Sirf Ye Soocho

Janaza Bhi Meri Madar Ka Uttha Raat Mai Logoo

Khulay Sar Kaisay Joao Mai Inn Galyou May Soocho, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



3.     Aseer-E-Zulm Houn Tashna Dahunn Houn Bewatan Mai Houn

Jo Apnay Bhai Per Subb Kuch Lota Day Wo Behn Mai Houn

Bohat Mazloom Houn Mai Meri Faryad Sunn Lo, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



4.     Ye Sar Abbas(A.s) Ka Naizay Pe Logoo Kyou Nahi Rukta

Kabhi Soocha Nahi Kya Ghum Hai Logoo Maajra Hai Kya

Behn Ki Beridaee Subbub Iss Ka Hai Logoo, Rida Dia Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)

5.     Tamasha Bunn Gayiee Zehra(S.a) Ki Beti Kya Ye Ghum Kum Hai

Akhi Ki Laash Say Bepurda Guzri Kya Ye Ghum Kum Hai

Mera Chera Na Dekho Tamasha Mutt Banao, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



6.     Inhee Gulyoo Mai Kul Baba Ka Meray Raaj Tha Logoo

Yahan Sai Berida Mai Kaisay Guzroo Buss Yehi Sochoo

Mai Choop Ho Ker Sahongi Saza Kuch Aur Dai Du, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



7.     Situm Wo Qulb Per Mai Nay Sahay Jo Dusht Mai Dhaye

Sahar Say Shaam Tukk Ghur Mera Ujjra Khaimay Julwaye

Ubb Itna Rehm Khao Meri Janib Na Dekho, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



8.     Sar-E-Naiza Meray Bhai Ka Sar Bhi Khoon Rota Hai

Mera Sajjad(A.s) Bhi Sar Ko Jhuka Ker Ghum Ye Sehta Hai

Wo Ghum Say Murr Na Jaye Buss Ek Ihsaan Ker Du, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)



9.     “Ali” Wo Shaam Ka Ghumnak Munzer Kya Likhay “Mazher”

Turup Ker Shaam Kay Logoo Say Boli Bint-E-Paighumber(S.a.w)

Saza-E-Mout Dai Du Mujhay Ya Phir Laenoo, Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi

(Aray O Shaam Walo Mai Saidani Houn Mojh Ko Rida Dai Du Dua Dongi)
