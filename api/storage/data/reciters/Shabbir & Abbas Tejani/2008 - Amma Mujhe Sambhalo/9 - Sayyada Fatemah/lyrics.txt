(Sayada Fatemah) X12

  1. Shohar Wali, Pidar Hai Payambar, Pisar Imam

Jis Bibi Ka Nabi Pe, Lazim Tha Ehtaraam

Ummat Ko Baad-E-Ehmad, Ayi Na Kyu(N) Haya

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  2. Duniya Ko Khalq Karke, Allah Ne Kaha,

Sara Jahaan Tere, Sadqe Me Hai Bana

Uncha Hai Kitna Bibi, Ye Martaba Tera

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  3. Bibi Tumhare Dar Se Humko Hai Aasra

Zainab Ka Waasta Hay Sunlijiye Dua

Miljaye Roze Hashr, Chadar Ki Bas Hawa

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  4. Kehti Rahi Mei Fatema, Bint-E-Rasool Hu(N)

Bagh-E-Fidak Ko Cheen Ke Khush Ho Rahe Ho Kyun

Bint-E-Rasool Ki Hai, Kya Hai Yahi Jaza

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  5. Tutti Huwi Kamar Hai, Mohsin Guzar Gaye

Ummat Ne Mujh Pe Baba Kya Kya Sitam Kiye

Qabre Rasool Par Yu(N) Karti Rahi Buka

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  6. Zainab Ko Ye Wasiyyat Deti Hai Fatema

Bazar-E-Sham O Kufa Ka Hoga Marhala,

Deene Khuda Ka Us Dam, Tum Hogi Aasra

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  7. Zahra Ka Laal Tha Aur, Zalim Ki Fauj Thi

Chaaro(N) Taraf Se Aayi, Barsaat Teer Ki,

Gore Se Jab Gire To, Sarwar Ne Di Sada

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  8. Balo(N) Se Saaf Karti, Zahra Woh Sar Zami(N)

Jis Khaak Pe Ghiregi, Shabbir Ki Jabi(N)

Kya Maa Ke Dil Pe Ghuzri, Jab Sheh Ka Sar Kataa

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  9. Beparda Qafla Jab, Aale Rasul Ka,

Karbobala Me Lut Ke, Zindan Ko Chala

Zainab Ko Har Qadam Par, Deti Thi Hosla

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8

  10. "Shabbir Ki Dua Hai" Abbas Ki Dua

Jeeteji Nawha Dono, Parhte Rahe Tera

Marte Huwe Bhi Lab Par, Bas Ho Yahi Sadaa

(Sayyada Fatemah) X8
