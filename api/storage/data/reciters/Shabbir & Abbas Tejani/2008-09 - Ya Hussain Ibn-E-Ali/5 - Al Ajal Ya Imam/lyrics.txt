Ayna Talibu Bi Dhuhulil Anbiya, Ayna Talibu Bi Damil Maqtooli Bi Karbala



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



1.     Har Taraf Jahan Me Zulm Aur Jafa Hai


Saare Momino Ke Lab Pe Ye Sada Hai

Inteqame Karbala Humko Dekhna Hai

Har Gari Hamare Dil Me Bus Dua Hai



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



2.     Ab Munfiqat Ka Dor Chal Raha Hai,

Bekhata Lahu Ko Jo Bahaa Raha Hai

Zulm Ki Hukumat, Aaj Har Jaga Hai

Samarra Me Dekho, Haye Kya Huwa Hai

Aaj Be Alaamat, Qabr-E-Sayyeda Hai



(Sayyeda Fatemah, Sayyeda Fatemah) X4



3.     Tum Bhi Mere Maula Ibne Sayyeda Ho


Maula Askari Kai, Aap Mehlaqa Ho

Aaj Aap Maula, Shaahe Karbala Ho

Zalimo(N) Ke Sar Ki, Aap Hi Qaza Ho



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



4.     Aap Ayiye Ab, Muntazir Hai Kabaa

Leke Awo Khanjar, Shahe La Fatah Ka

Aap Ayenge Jab, Inqelab Hoga

Aap Ka Hai Sadqa, Ye Jahaan Saara

Naam Leraha Hai, Har Malak Tumhara



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



5.     Parchame Hussaini Haath Me Uthaawo

Mausame Adalat, Aap Leke Aawo

Farsh Ab Aza Ka, Har Jaga Bichaawo

Aur Diya Aza Ka, Har Taraf Jalaawo

Rab Se Jo Kiya Hai, Wada Tum Nibhaawo



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



6.     Kaise Bhool Jaaye(N), Karbala Ka Manzar

Shaah-E-Karbala Tha, Zee(N) Pe Na Zamee(N) Par

Pyaasa Wo Gale Par, Chal Raha Tha Khanjar

Khayme Se Ye Zainab, Keh Rahi Thi Rokar

Babajaa(N) Bachaawo, Karbala Mei Aakar



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



7.     Darke Niche Zahra Jabke Gir Rahi Thi

Haath Tha Kamar Par Baap Ko Sada Di

Mohsine Ali Ko Jabke Kho Rahi Thi

Marte Marte Zahra Deti Thi Duhayi

Ab Hai Unki Badla Teri Zimmedari



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4



8.     Kayinaat Me Hai Har Zaba(N) Pe Naaraa

Inteqame Zahra Lijiye Khudaaraa,

Aap Ko “Tejani” Ne Hai Ab Pukaaraa

Dijiye Qalam Ko Aap Ka Sahaaraa

Nohay Jab Parhe Ham Saath Ho Tumhaaraa



(Alajal Ya Imam, Ya Imame Zamaan) X4
