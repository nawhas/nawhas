1.     Dasht karbal me tera, laash teeron pe para,

Rorahi thi ye zami(n), aasma(n) rota raha

Haaye madar wo teri, bayn ye karti rahi,

Ay mere pyaare hussain, god me aawo zara

(Waris-e-ilm-e-nabi, ya hussain ibn-e-ali, tere sadqe se mili, zindagi hamko

2.     Tere asghar ka gala, tere akbar ka jigar,

Bhaayi abbas ke gham, me teri tutti kamar,

Aur paamal badan, ran me qasim ka huwa

Eik din me hi luta, karbala me tera ghar

(Waris-e-ilm-e-nabi, ya hussain ibn-e-ali, tere sadqe se mili, zindagi hamko
yahi) x2



3.     Ran me talwar bina aaye abaase ali,

Har taraf hamla huwa, mashke paani bhi chidi,

Ankh me teer laga aur paywast huwa,

Dono bazu ko kata, ke gira asbe jari

Wa huwa kullu al-wafa’, yasrukhu bar wa samaa’,

saqat Abbas jari, min Hisaanin khashbi



(Waris-e-ilm-e-nabi, ya hussain ibn-e-ali, tere sadqe se mili, zindagi hamko
yahi) x2



4.     Haaye besheer tera, bezaba(n) qatl huwa,


Alqama roti rahi, larzi karbal ki zami(n)

Tumne jab uska lahu, apne chehre pe mala

(Waris-e-ilm-e-nabi, ya hussain ibn-e-ali, tere sadqe se mili, zindagi hamko

5.     Asr ashur ka waqt, kaise hum bhool sake

Ayi awaaze aza(n), aap gore se gire,

Khayme ke darpe teri, behn khu(n) roti rahi

Tha tera paak gala, shimr ke tegh tale

6.     Aag se khayme jale, sar se chaadar bhi chhini,

Dasht me tanha sakina teri roti rahi

Toq me bandha gaya, tere abid ka gala,

Aaj masjid se jo aati hai awaaze azaa(n)

Humko karbal ne sikhaya hai jine ka hunar,

Teri qurbaani se baqi hai ye arz-o-samaa(n)

Aapke paak badan, se huwa khun rawaa(n)
