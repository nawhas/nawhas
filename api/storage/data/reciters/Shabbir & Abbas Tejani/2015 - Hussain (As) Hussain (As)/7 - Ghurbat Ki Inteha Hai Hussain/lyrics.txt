

**_Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain_** ** __**

Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



1.     Hussain puri risaalat, bachaane waale hai 

Hussain wada-e-tifli, nibhaane waale hai

Haa(n) asl-e-aayat-e-tatheer ka tarjuma hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



2.     Badan hai teero(n) se chhalni, aur beet’ti hai qayamat 

Wo teen din ke hai pyaase, Hai aab ki kasrat

Jo sehn kar raha har zulm aur jafa Hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



3.     Hai yaad Khun mei dubi, jawaan ki mayyat 

Banayi haatho(n) se Asghar, ki jisne thi turbat

Wo jiske dil mei hazaaro(n) haa(n) gham jama hai hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



4.     Hussain ghazi ke laashe, pe aye dil thaame 

Kaha wo roke na rutho, ay bhaayi jaa(n) mujhse

Nahi hai munis-o-yawar faqat bacha hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



5.     Sawaar sine pe hai shimr, hai haath mei khanjar 

Gale pe sheh ki wo zalim, Chalaata hai hass kar

Sada falak se ye aayi zibah huwa hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



6.     Thi sham kaisi gharibo(n), pe jhal gaye khayme 

Pukaari roke ye zainab, wo dasht mei aake

Na mere sar pe bhi saaya koi bacha hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



7.     Ay Fatemah tere bête, ko ro rahe hai ham 

Wo jispe abid-e-muztar, na kar saka matam

Zabaa(n) pe aaj hamaare faqat sada hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain



8.     Nahi misaal muhabbat, ki phir kabhi aisi

“Tejani” sheh ki zabaa(n) par, thi bas sana rab ki

Allah karta hai khud hi jinki sana hai Hussain



Ghurbat ki inteha hai Hussain Ye deen ki baqa hai Hussain
