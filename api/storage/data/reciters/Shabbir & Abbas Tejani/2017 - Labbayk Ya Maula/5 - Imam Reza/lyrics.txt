Assalamu alayka ya ghareebal ghuraba

Assalamu alayka ya mu’eenadhu’afaai walfukaraa

Assalamu alayka ya sultan abal hassan

Aliyyabna musa ar-redhaa



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



1.     the land of mash'had, is like no other

we always leave with, our hearts desire

this is a guarantee, ask with sincerity

and you will see, that what will be

imam raza, call us to your side, you are our guide

when we've called you you have replied



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



2.     Is tarha aayi, tumhe shahaadat

The tum akayle, thi aisi ghurbat

Na chaahne waale, Janaaze pe aaye

Dono jahaan, Ki thi fughaan

Imam Raza, Aasmaan ka, Rang badal gaya

Tan se ruhoo jab juda huwa



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



3.     Aawmadam ay shaah panawham bideh

Khette amaawni zey gunawham bideh

Ay haramat mal jaw'edar mandegawn

Door marawn az daro rawham bideh

Imam Reza, eye gulle bikhaarey gulistaane ishq

Qorbe makawni chu giiyaham bideh



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



4.     Ya noora mash’had, qabran bi toosi

Mutta shaheeda, shamsa-shumoosi

Nabqi alayka sayyidi, yabnar-rasoolil amjadi

Ruhi fidaak, raghma idaak

Imam reda, lil haqqi qur’an, anta alish-shaan

Yabna an-nabiyyil mustafal haadi



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



5.     itna hai uncha, yeh dar tumhara

wohi bana hai, mera sahara

jab bhi parre mushkil, tumhe pukare dil

har hadisa, yun mit gaya

imam raza, aaj ho ya kal, karde bala hal

lab pe bus hai ye dua har pal



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



6.     you travelled so far, away from your home

you met your death when, you were so alone

your sister searched for you, and passed away in Qum

oh Fatima, Al masuma

imam raza, She could never bear, To be anywhere

In the world without you being there



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



7.     Laaw yeqqe vaasley to key man neestam

Ezn beyek lahzey nigawham bideh

Ay key hareemat masaley kahrowbawst

Shwqo sabowk kheeziye kawham bideh

Imam Reza, ta keze ishqe tu gudawzam chushaam (like sham marriage)

Garmiye jawn soowz be aawham bideh



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2



8.     Man zaara qabrak babul muraadi

Naalashafa’a ya abal jawadi

Sirrul imammati yash’hadi

Bil-mu’jizaati mukhalladi

Tashfil-aleel, ma’wad-dakheel

Imam Reda, lil insi wal-jaan, samawka sultaan

Yabna ar-rasoolil mustafal-haadi



(Imam Raza, waris-e-quraan, markaz-e-imaan, jaan-e-man shaah-e-khurasaan)x2
