_Allah, Ighfirli Khataee Wa Dhunoobee_

_Allah, Ultufbee Wastoor Li Ayoobee_

_Allah, Wa Taqabal Miman Yashfaooli_

_ _

(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



1.     Baba Tumhaare Baqir, Madar Hai Umm-E-Farwah

Rehmat Mei Sab Se Kasrat, Sarkaar Mei Ho Aala

Tum Shaam Ki Siyaahi, Ko Payrahan Banaakar

Har Raat Mufliso(N) Ko, Haatho(N) Se Hai Khilaya



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



2.     Tumhaari Aur Nabi Ki, Ik Roz Hai Wiladat

Aur Raah-E-Adl-O-Haq Mei, Thi Aapki Shahadat

Ahmed Ki Ruh Teri, Ruh Se Juri Huwi Hai

Saare Malak Pe Waajib, Hai Aapki Itaa’at



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



3.     Godi Hai Jinki Khaali, Awlaad Unhe Ataa Kar

Maqrooz Momino Ka, Jo Karz Ho Ada Kar

Hai Be Khata Jo Qaydi, Dede Unhe Bashaarat

Karte Hai Ham Tawassul, Haatho(N) Ko Ab Utha Kar



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



4.     Bechayn Ye Jigar Hai, Ham Tumse Jo Juda Hai

Yasrab Ko Tum Bulaalo, Lab Pe Yahi Dua Hai

Aake Kare Ziyarat, Aur Ashk Ham Bahaaye

Tere Liye Ay Maula, Ye Dil Tarap Raha Hai



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



5.     Tumko Sata Raha Tha, Ashur Ka Wo Manzar

Ik Behn Ne Jo Dekha, Ik Bhayi Zeyr-E-Khanjar

Pyaara Sa Uska Chehra, Dekha Tha Khun Mei Tar

Rokar Wo Gir Pari Jab, Tan Se Juda Huwa Sar



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



6.     Angur Ka Gahal Jab, Mansur Tumko Bheja

Tab Sakht Zehr Usne, Angur Mei Tha Daala

Khaakar Laraz Raha Tha, Maula Badan Tumhara

Girya Kiya Uthaakar, Kazim Tumhaara Lasha



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2



7.     Baade Teri Shahadat, Duniya Mei Aaj Tak Bhi

Zahir Tera Asar Hai, Roshan Khuda Ke Raah Ki

Dam Se Tumhaare Qayam, Iman Jaafari Ka

Shiyyo(N) Ka Fakhr Tum Ho, Bole Na Kyu(N) “Tejani”



(Jaffar-E-Sadiq, Jaffar-E-Sadiq) X2
