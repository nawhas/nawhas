Haider Ne Namaazo Me Jo Allah Se Manga

Kehte The Khuda De Mujhe Abbas Sa Beta

Wo Jisko Kahe Sara Arab Ghaziyo Jarrar



(Abbas-E-Alamdaar, Abbas-E-Alamdaar) X2



1.     Tum Bazuwe Shabbir Ho Haider Ke Pisar Ho

Lashkar Ke Alamdar Sakina Ka Jigar Ho

Jitey Hai(N) Tumhe(N) Dekh Ke Bas Sayyede Abraar



(Abbas-E-Alamdaar, Abbas-E-Alamdaar) X2



2.     Paani Pe Alam Tere Siwaa Kisne Lagaya

Mashkize Me Darya Ko Tumhi Ne Hai Uthaya

Tum Lashkar-E-Shabbir Ke Ho Haider-E-Karrar



(Abbas-E-Alamdaar, Abbas-E-Alamdaar) X2



3.     Tum Fatema Zehra Ki Duwawo Ka Asar Ho

Tum Dhoop Me Zainab Ke Liye Misle Shajar Ho

Parcham Ka Pharayra Hai Tera Aisa Hawaadar



(Abbas-E-Alamdaar, Abbas-E-Alamdaar) X2



Ya Abbas



4.     Zainab Ne Dam Karke Naade Ali,

Apni Rida Bhayi Par Daaldi,

Apni Rida Bhayi Par Daaldi

Dekar Duwa Ghar Se Rukhsat Kiya

Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda



(Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda) X4



5.     Ghazi Ne Saahil Pe Rakha Qadam,

Khakar Sakina Ke Sar Ki Qasam,

Bharlunga Is Mashk Me Alqama

Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda



(Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda) X4



6.     Lekin Achanak Ye Kya Hogaya,

Ghazi Ka Parcham Larazne Lagaa,

Bedast Hokar Giraa Bawafaa

Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda



(Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda) X4



7.     Ghazi Jo Zee(N) Se Zami(N) Par Ghira

Rokar Sakina Se Usne Kaha

Sharminda Bali Tera Hai Chacha

Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda



(Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda) X4



8.     "Rehan" Manzar Wo Kaise Likhu(N)

Mere Qalam Se Tapak Ta Hai Khu(N)

Kehte The Shabbir Bhayi Mera

Abbas Ibne Shere Khuda



(Abbas Ibn-E-Sher-E-Khuda) X4
