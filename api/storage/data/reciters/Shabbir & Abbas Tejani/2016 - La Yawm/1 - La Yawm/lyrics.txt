Hussain, Hussain, Hussain



Assalamu Alayka Ya Aba Abdillah

Assalamu Alayka Ya Sayyedish-Shuhadaa



(La Yawma Kayawmak Ya Hussain) X4

    1.     Your Broken And Shattered Heart


    On That Day Was Ripped Apart


    Your Body Wounded And Scarred


    Stranded From Your Home So Far


    Hussain, Your Pain, Was Not, In Vain


    Please Call Us To You Soon, Ya Hussain


    So That We See Your Tomb, Ya Hussain

(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2

    2.     Ishqu Hayaati Hussain, Nabadu Fuaadi Al-Daami


    Dhikruka Yabqaa Hussain, Qatau Ra'saka Dhaami


    An'aak, Ahwaak, Wiyaak, Wiyaak


    Hatta Al-Mamaati Ma'ak Ya Hussain


    Kullu Hayaati Ma'ak Ya Hussain

(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2

    3.     Ragrag Me Mere Basta


    Hai Ishq Ka Ye Dariya


    Wo Jiska Ik Ik Qatra


    Parhta Hai Naam Tumhara


    Ye Gham, Matam, Har Dam, Payhum


    Hai Jaan Se Pyara Ya Hussain


    Hai Khun, Hai Khun, Hai Khun Mei Behta

(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2



4.     There Is No Day Like Your Day
Until We Die We Will Say
Let Us Live Life In Your Way
From Your Path Don't Let Us Stray
Today, The Skies, Echo, Your Cries
In Your Love I'm Insane
'Til Death I'll Call Your Name



(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2



5.     Karbobala Ka Hai Ban

Hussain Hai Khastatan

Khanjar Tale Hai Gardan

Lahu Mei Duba Hai Ran

Quraan, Ki Jaan, Noke Sinaan

Hai Aaj Tak Rota Ya Hussain

Jahaan Ye Sara Ya Hussain



(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2



6.     Qatalu Al-Haqqa Shaheeda,

Ayna Yazeedi Al-Taaghi

Saara Imaami Nasheeda,

Dhidda Al-Dhulmi Al-Baaghi

Qurbaan, Quraan, Ahzaan Ahzaan

Shamsuka Baaqi Abad Ya Hussain

Nahjuka Saami Abad Ya Hussain



(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2



7.     Laying Is The Prophets Rose
Upon A Bed Of Arrows
From Your Body The Blood Flows
Your Pain Only Allah Knows
Your Blood, Was Shed, Islam, Was Spread
The Saviour Of This Deen
Your Love Has Set Me Free



(La Yawm Ka Yawmak Ya Hussain) X2



8.     "Tejani" Ka Hai Wada
Ta Hashr Tak Ae Maula
Karenge Hum Ye Girya
Dete Rahenge Pursa
Har Aan, Ye Jaan, Tujpar, Qurbaan
Hain Hum Tere Nokar
Bus Hai Sada Lab Par
