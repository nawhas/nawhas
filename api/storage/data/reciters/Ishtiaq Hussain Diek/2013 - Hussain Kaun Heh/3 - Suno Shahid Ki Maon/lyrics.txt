Suno Shahid Ki Maon  Ye Fatima Ka Bayan

Ke Degi Hashr Mein Uska Sila Hussain Ki Maan



Rahe Hussain Mein Bête Tumahare Mare Gaye

Tho Sidae Chashmae Kausar Ke Vo Kinare Gaye

Aur Unke Waste Qasr E Jinan Savare Gaye

Diya Heh Khuld Mein Un Sab Ko La Makan Ne Makan



Kisi Ka Lal Tho Karyal Jawan Mara Gya

Kisi Ka Lal Koi Be Zaban Mara Gya

Kisi Ke Ghar Ka Koi Paasbaan Mara Gya

Tumhare Rone Pe Ro Deti Heh Hussain Ki Maan



Kisi Jawan Ki Jo Mayet Utayee Jate Heh

Tho Us Ghari Mujhe Akbar Ki Yaad Ate Heh

Aur Um Laila Ye Us Dam Sada Sunati Heh

Ab In Ko Sabr Ata Karde Hussain Ki Man



Tumharee Goad Ke Kam Sin Diyae Bhujae Gaye

Aur Un Ke Khoon Bhare Lashe Tumhein Dikhae Gai

Ye Sab Hamari Muhabbat Mein Zulm Dhayae Gai

Tho Hum Hi Lein Ge Bas Ab Inteqam Uska Yahan

Tumhare Dil Pe Guzarte Heh Jo Mujhe Heh Khabr

Jinan Mein Men Bhi Tumhare Liye Hoon Noha Gar

Jo Barpa Hoga Kabhi Yaum E Hashr Ka Manzar

Ye Mera Wa’da Heh Dilwaoon Gi Mein Tum Ko Jinaan



Han Dekha Mein Ne Bhi Katthe Hue Hussain Ka Sar

Mein Kya Bataon Ke Guzri Thi Kya Mere Dil Par

Mein Rakh Rahee Thi Khud Apna Gala Tehae Khanjar

Magar Hussain Ka Sar Agaya Ban Ok E Sinan



Mujhe To Qabr Mein Bhi Chain Se Rehne Diya

Baqi Mein Mere Roze Ko Bhi Giraya Gya

Jahan Mein Akhri Beta Mera Jo Aye Ga

Tho Inteqam Hare K Zulm Ka Le Ga Yahan



Bata Do Majid O Diek Ba Sadqa Abbas

Dua E Fatima E Zahra Heh Matmi Ki Asaas

Hamare Qalb Mein Ye Jagta Rahe Ihsaas

Vo Kamyab Heh Jo Shabbier Par Ho Qurban



Poet :- Allama Majid Raza Abidi, Noha Khawan:- Ishtiaq Hussain Diek, Soz:-
Farhan Ali
