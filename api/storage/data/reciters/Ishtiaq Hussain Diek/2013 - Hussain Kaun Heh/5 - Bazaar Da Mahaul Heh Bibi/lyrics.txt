_Haule_ _ haule qadam wada bachra_

_ _

Tez turr den rasi chekendee ae

Sadi halat te kar niga bachra



Tu ubale qadam jo chenda hein, ae kamene zulm karende hin

Chote chote yatim balan kun nal Koran de ae marinade hin

Wasta hi bhein di yateemi da  apni raftaar kun ghata bachra



Tekun parde da dard dukhenda ae, mekun ae soch vendi khandi ae

Bheer bazaar vich batulan te maut di zardi vendi jande ae

Ke karesan jekar batul koi, kar gai bazaar wich qaza bachra



Thi ke majboor jaen wele mawan,   apne sar kun raku tun chendeya in

Chote chote masum qaidiyan diyan, hai talyan  pech o pendiyan hin

Kya in halat wich o tur sangden, das ae ghairat da badshah bachra



Asan ae jai ghareeb qaidi hin, charon pason jinan de qatil hin

Tun hiko asra  hein ujriyan da, tedi maran uthe vi mail hin

Kar na sadi fikr tun uveen tur, jeven  qatil di riza bachra

Akhay irshad sain abid kun , tendi akiyan choon nir kyun lir den

Tu evay janree aj bazaar de wich, sadey chauhat mayyet

Kya hoya chadran jo nai dehendae, sade sir di hein tu rida bachra
