Matam Yazidiyat Ke Khilaf Ihtijaj Heh

Is Ka Jawab Koi Tha Kal Aur Na Aaj Heh



Elan Ker Raha Hy Yah Matam Hussain As Ka

Kar O Bala Ka Sare Zamane Pe Raj Heh



Sajad Ne Masle Pe Ro Kar Bata Diya

Matam Ibaton Ke Liye Misl E Taaj Heh



Ata Nahein Yaqeen Tho Khak E Shifa Se Pucho

Har La Dwaa Ka Elaj Matam Elaj Heh



Matam Namaz Ek Hein Hargiz Alag Nahein

Donon Ka Kibriya Ki Qasam Ek Mizaj Heh



Ruh E Hayat Heh, Ye Hararat E Khoon Heh

Matam Nahein Jis Mein Vo Kesa Samaj Heh



Allah Ka Wa’da Tamanna Batul Ki

Kis Ne Ye Keh Diya Ke Matam Riwaj Nahein



Kuch Bhi Nahein Heh Aur Shifa’at Ke Waste

Matam Ke Hath Mir Takallum Ki Laaj Hey

Poet:- Mir Takallum, Noha Khan:- Ishtiaq Hussain, Soz:- Wahji Hasan
