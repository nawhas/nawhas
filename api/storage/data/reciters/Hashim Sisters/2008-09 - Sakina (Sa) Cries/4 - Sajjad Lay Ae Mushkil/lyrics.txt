(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil


2.     Hoya riha jadown da, chup chup Sajaad reynda

Puche haal koyi roke, na Shaam da ay laynda

(Jeray zulm vekhay Abid) x2 duniya nu ki khabar



(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil


3.     Asghar da kurta veeran, meri laid vich rakhade

Ehsan kaar kabaar da, Abid nishan mita de

(Tere baad ana javei) x2 meri kabaar te Shimar.



(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil


4.     Tatheer jo waris, aj hoyi bay rida ay

(Sir nanga vekh tera) x2 mera phat gaya jigar.



(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil


5.     Akhay Rubab Abid, mushkil hai mera jeena

(Hoyee ghod meri khali) x2 kis de laagi nazar


(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil



6.     Ais Shaam day sheher vich, Shabbir rul gayee yaan

(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil



7.     Ronda ai khoon kaidi, nai marz koyi puraanee

(Sajaad lay ae mushkil, aey Shaam da safar) x2 Sajaad lay ae mushkil
