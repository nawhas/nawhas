(Haay Abbas, Haay Abbas) X4



(Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai) X2



1.     Kon Khaymo(N) Ko Jhalayega Bhala

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



2.     Meray Abbas Ka Mashoor Hai Ye Jaho Hasham

Mera Abbas To Hai Aal-E-Mohammed Ka Bharam

Jaantay Hai Ye Sabhi Ehle Jafa

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



3.     Sani-E-Haider-E-Karar Hai Ghazi Mera

Sheh Kay Lashkar Ka Alamdar Hai Ghazi Mera

Meri Madar, Meray Baba Ki Duaa

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



4.     Chor Kar Apna Watan Iskay Saharay Aai

Thi Khabar Saath Hai Abbas Sa Mera Bhai

Mujhko Kis Baat Ki Ho Fikr Bhala

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



5.     Kiski Jurrat Hai Jo Khaimo Ki Taraf Daikh Sakay

Kon Aisa Hai Jo Akar Meri Chadar Cheenay

Hath Katjaey Ga Jo Aagay Barha

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



6.     Hai Shaqi Kon Sakina Kay Jo Dur Cheensakay

Hai Laeen Kon Jo Bachi Ko Tamachay Maray

Meri Masoom Sakina Ka Chacha

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



7.     Dushman-E-Sheh Kay Lahu Say Yeh Dubodaiga Jahan

Mil Na Paey Gi Sitamgar Ko Kahin Jaey-Amma

Sheh Ko Bekas Na Samajhna Ada

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



Kon Cheenay Ga Meray Sar Say Rida, Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai



8.     Sheh Nay Talwar Uthanay Ki Ijazat Bhi Na Di

Safdar-E-Khasta Jigar Qatl Hua Sher-E-Jari

Bekhabar Deti Thi Zainab Ye Sada

Mera Abbas Abhi Zinda Hai
