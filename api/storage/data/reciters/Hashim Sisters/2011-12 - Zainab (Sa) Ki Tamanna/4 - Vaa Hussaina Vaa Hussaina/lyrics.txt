Nok-E-Naiza Kuja Hussain Kuja\---------- Meray Husssain Ka Sar



Ya Khuda Kaisi Woh Qayyamat Thi

Meray Bachay Pay Jo Museebat Thi

Laash-E-Besar Baghair Turbat Thi

Sar Charhay Gaya Sarre Naiza



Aah Naizo Pay Sar Jo Chaltay Thay

Kabhi Girtay Kabhi Sambhaltay Thay

Kabhi Naaqon Say Woh Kuchaltay Thay

Khoon Rota Raha Khudae Wafa



Bosa Gaahe Ali O Paighambar

Markaze Hasrate Dille Shabbar

Mera Quraan Aur Khaak Basar

Kia Yehi Ajr Hai Risalat Ka



Bola Darbaar Mein Koi Aakar

Laya Hoon Tuhfae Sarre Sarwar

Phatt Gaya Ghum Say Qalbe Paighambar

Farq E Sarwar Kuja Yazeed Kuja



Run Ka Aankhon Mein Woh Samaan Jaisay

Zad Pay Taighon Ki Ho Yeh Jaan Jaisay

Aur Ceenay Mein Ho Cina Jaisay

Kaisay Bhoolon Mein Apni Karbobala



Tasht Mein Sar Tha Takht Par Naari

Lab Pay Zaalim Pay Jab Charri Maari

Koi Chaara Na Tha Siwa Zaari

Mera Beemar Kaanp Kaanp Utha



Kia Adawat Hai Kaisi Saazish Hai

Aal Say Bughz Ki Numaaish Hai

Sar Hai Aur Patharo Ki Bearish Hai

Chaand Mera Lahoo Mein Doob Gaya



Aey Nigar Ashk Ro Raha Hai Qalam

Hashimi Dukhtaro Mein Hai Matam

Zakhme Zehra Ka Kuch To Ho Marham

Wa Hussaina Ki Aarahi Hai Sada



Vaa Hussaina Vaa Hussaina

Vaa Hussaina Vaa Hussaina
