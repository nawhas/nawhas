Dunya ko payaam-e-shah-e-mazloom sunaadey

Maatam ki sada se dil-e-baatil ko hilaadey

Ghar ghar me saff-e-maatam shabbit bichaadey



Sab bola ya hussain (as)



Zainab ki tamanna zainab ki tamanna

(1) aey sheh kay azadaro ye gham bhool na jana
dunya main kisi ranj o museebat ko jo pana
han farsh e aza e gham e shabbir bichana
jab ashk bahay ga ... Koi gham na rahay ga
is gham ko hi seenay say lagana

(2) zainab ki sada aaj bhi ati hai lahad say
shabbir ko jee khol kay hum ro nahi paye
aey sheh kay azadaro hain jo ashk tumharay
ye ashk tumharay ... Marham hain hamaray
har gham ki dawa ashk bahana

(3) zainab ki tamanna hai kay tum ashk bahao
farsh e gham e shabbir ko har ghar main bichao
ro kar shah e mazloom ka paigham sunao
ashkon ka ye toofan ... Hai fatha ka ailan
yuun deen ki tauqeer barhana

(4) ye rasm e aza uswa e masoom hai logo!
Ye rasm e aza deen ka maqsoom hai logo!
Ye rasm e aza nusrat e mazloom hai logo!
Jo seena zuni hai ... Naizay ki ani hai
naiza dil e batil ko lagana

(5) ye noha o matam na tashddud na junoo hai
ye farsh e aza qalb e sakina ka sukoon hai
han aaj bhi kurtay pay laga kano ka khoon hai
deti hai sadaain ... Aey ammu ab aaein
gham meri sakina ka batana





“hum bibi ki khwahish ko sada yad rakhaingay
ye farshe aza hashr tak abaad rakhingay”

(6) hum bibi ki khwahish ko sada yad rakhaingay
ye farshe aza hashr tak abaad rakhingay
jism ho jaye dopara
khoon bah jaye hamara
farsh e majlis ko basad shan bichaingay hum





(7) hum farsh e aza dekhaingay kaabay ki tarha say
aur is ko bichaingay musallay ki tarha say
is pay khud ati hain zahra
ashk barsati hain zahra
apni bibi ki tarha ashk bahaingay hum


(8) hum jaantay hain deen ka naara hai ye matam
haan maadar e zainab ki tamanna hai ye matam
anbia kartay hain matam
aulia kartay hain matam
apnay rutbay isi matam say barhaingay hum
zainabi jazba.....



meri shehzadi aey zainab , tum say hum sab ki dua hai
hum ko aey dukhtare zahra , tum kaneez apni banana
tum ko asghar ki qasam hai

khoon e akbar ki qasam hai ,
qom kay bacchon ko bibi , tum azadar banana

mola abbas k tum ko , katay hathon ki qasam hai
sab jawanon ko aey bibi , tum alamdaar banana
taazia jab bhi sajaaein , farsh e gham jab bhi bicchaaein
lay k sab ahl e haram ko , farsh e majlis pa tum aana
tum ko zahra ki qasam hai , apnay baba ki qasam hai
hum azadaron k dil main , hubb e shabbir barhaana
jo qama ka karain matam , dain jo zanjeer ka pursa
bibi zahra ki nazar main , sab ko maqbool banana
apna ye noha o matam , apni ye girya o zari
tum hamaray dil o jan main , aur ye jazba barhana
kartay hain shukria tera , ye karam hai teray darr ka
mil gaya hum ko ye rutba , noha khwaan kahti hai dunya



aey mahum-o-adeeya-o-saania keh do
zainab ki ata say ye mila martaba hum ko
mil gaya hum ko ye rutba
noha khwan kahti hai dunya
aur qayamat mein bhi majid yun hi jaaengey hum
