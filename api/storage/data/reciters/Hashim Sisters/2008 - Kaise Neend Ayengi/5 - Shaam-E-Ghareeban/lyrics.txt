Teray azaaim keh mei qurbaan, behna teri himat ko salaam

khatm hui hai karbobala aur, ab hai tera bas aik hi kaam

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  1. Jaey gi jahan beparda tu (naizay pe chalay ga sar mera) x2

ab to hi muhafiz hai zainab (hai teray hawalay ghar mera) x2

Quraan sunata jaeyga sar aaj say naizay par mera

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  2. Kuch dair main aa kar chenain gay (ye ehl-e-sitam teri chadar) x2

khaimo ko jalaein gay aada (balway mein phiraien gay dar dar) x2

koi bhi museebat aajaey par sabr tujhay karna hai magar

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  3. har gaam pe aey meri behna (tufan-e-masaib aaengay) x2

bazaron mein darbaron mein (yeh ehl-e-sitam lay jaengay) x2

beparda haram ko ye zalim chadar keh lieay tarsaengay

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  4. Sajjad mera qaidi ban kar (zanjeer pehan kar jaey ga) x2

beparda harm ko dekhay ga (ankhon say lahu barsaeyga) x2

seh lay ga sitam har aik magar shikwa na zaban par laiey ga

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  5. chenain gay ridaieyn naizon say (hathon mein rasan badhain gay laeen) x2

pardais mein nana ki ummat (kar day gi haram pe tang zameen) x2

bedard zamanay mein behna koi bhi tera humdard nahin

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  6. nana say kiya wada main nay (wada wo wafa kar day zainab) x2

is deen-e-khuda ki khatir tu (ghar bar fida kar day zainab) x2

ta hashr zamana yaad rakhay haq aisa ada kar day zainab

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  7. Awlaad-e-nabi par aey behna (wo waqt-e-qayamat aeayga) x2

Rahoon main tamashaai hongay (bazar sajaya jaey ga) x2

marnay ki duaa mango gi jahan ye waqt wahan par layega

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  8. barsaein gay pathar zalim (yoon hoga istiqbaal tera) x2

naizay pe mera sar roay ga (us waqt jo hoga haal tera) x2

darbar-e-sitam say zindan tak ro ro kay katay ga sal tera

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2

  9. paband-e-rasan hamsheer chalay (naizay pe chala bhai ka sar) x2

bhai ko kafan bhi mil na saka (khuwhar kay chini sar say chadar) x2

"Safdar" ye sadaein suntay huay zainab nay kiya aaghaz-e-saf

(sham-e-ghareeban agai zainab, agay manzil shaam ki hai) x2
