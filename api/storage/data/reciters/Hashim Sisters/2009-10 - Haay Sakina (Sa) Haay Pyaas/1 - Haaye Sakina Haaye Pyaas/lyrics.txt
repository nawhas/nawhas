Khud Ye Alam Bhi Kehta Hai Haaye Sakina Haaye Pyaas

(Haay Sakina Haaye Pyaas) X4



Ro Ke Deti Thi Sakina Ye Sada (Ab Hame Pyaas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



1.     Piyasay Bacchon K Labon Par Hai Buka Ab Hame Pias Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



2.     Haey Wo Piyasay Bacchay, Ashk Barsanay Lagay

(Nannay Hathon Main Jo Thay, Koozay Sab Tor Diye) X2

Aur Parcham Say Lipat Kar Ye Kaha, Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



3.     Kabhi Sar Ko Peeta, Kabhi Seena Peeta

(Hai Labon Par Ye Sada, Jald Aa Jao Chacha) X2

Ab Na Pani Ka Karaingay Shikwa, Ab Hame Pyaas Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



4.     Aey Meray Acchay Chacha, Aey Meray Pyaray Chacha

(Aey Meray Pyasay Chacha, Meray Dukh Paey Chacha) X2

Ab Koi Lutf Na Jeenay Main Raha, Ab Hame Pyaas Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



5.     Aap K Baad Chacha, Zulm Hum Par Ye Hua

(Kisi Ka Hath Bandha, Kisi Ka Nannha Gala) X2

Ab To Be Mashk Hi Aa Jao Chacha, Ab Hame Pyaas Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



6.     Gham Zada Wo Bacchay, Jis Ghari Qaid Huay

(Sailian Khatay Huay, Bas Yehi Kahtay Thay) X2

Pani Mut Lao Tum Aa Jao Chacha, Ab Hame Pyaas Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2



7.     Aa K Tum Dekho Zara, Ham Pe Jo Zulm Huwa

(Pani Jab Bhi Manga, Zalimo Nay Mara) X2

Tum Batao Ye Laeeno Ko Zara, Ab Hame Pyaas Nahi Hai Ammu



(Ab Hame Piyas Nahi Hai Ammu) X2
