Das Nana Main Ujri Noo, Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey

Teray Rozay Tay Aa Kar Ni Aan… Har Roz Ay Sawaal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….



1.     Main Khuwab Daraunay Vekhay, Main Darkay Rondi Raaey Aa

Main Khoon Da Vekhayaa Darya Ay (2)

Haey Jis Vich Rull Gaey Aal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….



2.     Ik Saal Musalsal Guzargaya, Main Veer Da Chehra Vekheya Nai

Maino Akbar Veer Milla Nana

Jera Khud Be Misul Misaal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….



3.     Kadmaan Dey Nishana Noo Tak Kay, Har Velay Rowaan Nana

Sughra Noo Har Dam Ownda Ay

Vichray Veeraan Da Khayaal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….



4.     Main Vekhi Khuwab Day Vich Nana, Ik Khoon Alooda Maqtal Gah

Maqtool Da Laasha Jis Day Vich

Paya Ruj Ya Teeran Naal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….



5.     Jinoon Tak Kay Meray Dukhriyaan Vich, Hojanda Haur Izafa Ay

Char Aaya Vekh Moharram Da

Aj Nana Fir O Hilaal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….



6.     Diek Ali Akbar Da Matam Dhian Hashim Kar Kay Rondia Ey

Kar Da Ay Duawa Una Day Lieay

Haey Paak Batool Da Laal Ey

Meray Veer Da Ki Haal Ey….
