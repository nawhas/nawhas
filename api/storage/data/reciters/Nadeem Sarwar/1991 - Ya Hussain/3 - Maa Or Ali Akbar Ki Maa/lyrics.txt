Jab Ali Akbar Se Beta, Shah-E Ko Rab Ne Diya. Is Ataaye Kibriya Par, Karbala
Ne Di Sadaa.

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

1. Uska Matha Chum Tithi Ume Layla Bar Bar,

Uska Chehra Dekh Kar Kehtithi Ay Parwardigar,

Ye Bahare Zindagi Hai, Ye Mere Gar Ki Bahaa,

Ye Meri Pehli Tamana, Ye Mere Dil Ka Karaar.

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

2. Kali Dori Bandti Nani Kalaai Me Kabhi,

Nane Sine Par Kabhi Kar Tithi Dam Nade Ali,

Kehti Thi Murjaye Na Maula Mere Dil Ki Kali,

Ye Dile Shabbir Hai, Hai Hamari Zindagi

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

3. Gutniyo Chalne Laga Jis Waqt Unka Sheerkhwaar,

Saaf Karti Apne Anchal Se Wo Uski Reh Guzar,

Eh Khuda Na Khaasta Chub Jaye Na Bache Ki Khaal,

Chalte Chalte Jab Wo Thakta Pusht Pe Karti Sawaar

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

4. Khayr Se Jab Umr Bhar Kar Uska Is Kabul Huwi,

Jis Me Gehware Ki Khahish Ho Na Maa Ki God Ki,

Bachpana Rukhsat Talab Ho Aur Shabab Ahmed Ghari,

Jab Jawani Aa Rahi Ho Aur Masi Ho Bhigti

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

5. Sab Kunu Ne Harb Sikla Te The Abbase Jari,

Sehn Me Khursi Bicha Kar Maa Bhi Aksar Dekhti,

Mashk Ke Doran Ek Din Yuhi Neze Ki Ani,

Chu Gayi Sine Se Layla Par Ghashita Ri Huwi

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

6. Kya Khabar Thi Umme Layla Ke Wo Din Bhi Ayenga,

Byah Ka Arman Dil Ka Dil Me Hi Rehjayenga,

Laashe Qasim Ki Tarha Tukro Me Dil Katjayenga,

Nawjawaan Ki Laash Par Sehra Sajaa Ya Jayenga

(Maa Or Ali Akbar Ki Maa) X2

7. Umme Layla Dil Sambalo Arsh Se Aayi Sadaa,

Barchiyo Ki Simtey Jaata Hai Tumhara Meh Lakaa,

Ye Karo Ruksat Ke Jaise Ho Tamam Baraat Ka,

Khak Me Miljayengi Nana Shabi Ho Mustufa
