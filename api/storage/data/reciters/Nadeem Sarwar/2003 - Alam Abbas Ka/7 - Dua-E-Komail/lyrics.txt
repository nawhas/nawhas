  1. Tu hi kabir tu hi kibriya tu rehnuma, Rage gulu se bhi nazdeek tu har ek lamha, Hai tere naam ki taasir har maraz ki dawa, Hai tere zikr me poshida mere hak me shafa, (baghair maange hi karta hai tu ataa pe ataa) x2 Sadaa se pehle hi sunta hai sahilo ki sada

(ya sari ar reza) x2 Ighfir leman la yamleko ilad dua, Fa innaka fa'alool lema

  3. Mere khuda wo zabaane jo teri hamd kare, Wo tere panjatan-e-paak ki dua me rahe, Kabhi wo nare jahanum ki sakhtiya na sahe, Pule siraat se bekhof bekhatar guzre, (gunahagaro ko ye aasra hai subho masa) x2 khula huwa hai musalsal tera dare tawba

(ya sari ar reza) x2 Ighfir leman la yamleko ilad dua, Fa innaka fa'alool lema
