Aa ali Asghar

**_

(Aa Ali Asghar)x2

(Tujhko jo pawoo mere Asghar) x2 Jula julao aa mere Asghar.

Jula julao lori sunao dood pilao aa mere Asghar.

Tujko jo paao aa mere Asghar.



  1. Mere mujahid mere sipahi (Khatme huwi mein dame larayi) x2

Ghar ko sidharo maa ko pukaro. Mein chein pawu aa mere Asghar. Jula julao…



  2. Yaad arahi hein masum bete (Din mein sukun hai bechein raate) x2 Ji na sakungi roti rahungi. Ansu bahao aa mere Asghar. Jula julao…



3\. Puch rahi hai bali Sakina (Kyu nahi aya lot ke bhayya) x2 To hi batade
maidan se aake. Mein kya batawu aa mere Asghar. Jula julao…



4\. Pehre huwe hai Shaam ke saye (Ab to parinde bhi lot aaye) x2 Mein rorahi
hu jaan ko rahi ho. Tujko sulao aa mere Asghar. Jula julao…

5\. Mehsus mujko ye ho raha hai (Jese ke jula bhi roraha hai) x2 Ay mere beta,
ye rota jula. Kaise julawu aa mere asghar. Jula julao…



6\. Maktal se ayi aise khabar (Khurta lahu sab tera tar hai) x2 Teer laga hai
zakhmi gala hai. Mein marna jawo aa mere Asghar. Jula julao…
