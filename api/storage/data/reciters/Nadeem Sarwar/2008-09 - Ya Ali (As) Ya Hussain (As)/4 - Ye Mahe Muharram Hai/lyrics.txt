(Arze Karbala Arze Nainawa) X2

(Arze Arze Karabala) X3 Ay Arze Nainawa



(Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He)



1.     Is Maah Mein Luti Gayi Zehra Ki Kamayi
Talwar Muhammad Ke Nawase Pe Chalayi
Is Maah Mein Jibreel Se Nauha Ye Suna He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



2.     Is Maahe Muharram Ne Qayamat Ye Dikhayi
Is Chaand Ne Sughra Ko Khabar Jaake Sunayi
Veeran Madina He Basi Karbobala He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



3.     Is Maah Mein Saadaat Ne Din Kaise Guzare
Masum Ko Pani Na Mila Nehr Kanare
Kya Zulm Muhammad Ke Gharane Ne Saha He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



4.     Jab Chaand Muharram Ka Nazar Aata He Humko
Sajda He Azaakhana To Yaad Ataa He Humko
Is Chaand Mein Khu(N) Aale Muhammad Ka Baha He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



5.     Aashur Ka Din Bhool Na Paayega Zamaana
Luta Gaya Us Roz Muhammad Ka Gharana
Us Roz Se Dunya Mein Bichhi Farshe Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



6.     Kya Hosle Maaon Ke The Kya Sher The Bachhe
Woh Maqsade Zehra Thi Ye Haidar Ke Iraade
In Maaon Se Bachhon Se Bani Khake Shifa He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



7.     Wo Auno Muhammad Ali Akbar Ali Asghar
Wo Qasime Naushah Woh Abas-E-Dilawar
Maqtal Inhi Shehzadon Ke Zakhmon Se Saja He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



8.     (Ho “Sarwaro Rehan Shanawar Ke Ali Jee”) X2
He Naukri Virse Me Hussain Ibne Ali Ki
Awaazo Qalam Donon Isi Dar Ki Ata He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



Ye Maahe Muharram He Ye Ayyame Aza He
Matam Ye Muhammad Ke Nawaase Ka Bapa He



(Arze Arze Karabala) X3 Ay Arze Nainawa
