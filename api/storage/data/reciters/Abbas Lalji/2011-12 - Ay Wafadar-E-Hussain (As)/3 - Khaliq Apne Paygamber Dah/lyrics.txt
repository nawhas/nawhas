Khaliq Apney Paygambar Dah Ik Waar Zahoor Wala Kar, Meku Akbar Putar Ata Kar

Mehrooma Ma Da Putar Han’ May, Mendi Ay Manzoor Dua Kar
Maiku Akbar Putar Ata Kar

1) Menda Nana Tur Gaya Duniya Tu(N) , Odhey Roop Da Jald Zahoor Hovay ,
Meku Dey Farzand Rasool Jeya, Menda Bachra Ain Darood Hovai,
Sada Haq Koi Devey Na Devey, Tu Apna Khums Ada Kar.

2) Ay Khaliq Vaikh Hussain Ha Mey, Wada Har Haal Nibha Sakdah,
Maslain Day Sona Bachray Da Mey Tahtul Halak Wala Sakda,
Pyu Putar Di Laash Kiven Chenda, Karbal To Aap Nigah Kar.

3) Mendi Bhain Di Hasrat Hai Khaliq, Oku Burkaiyan Day Wich Chupa Lar’ndi,
Mey Zimmawari Chahna Ha’n, Odhi Lash Koo Aap Sambhalan’di
Odi Ahmad Naal Jahan Ootay Mendi Surkhuru Karbobala Kar.

4) Rat Rovan Walay Shaam Dai Wich, Menda Aabid Putar Beemar Sahi,
Akbar Bas Saal Athara Da, Menday Wehray Dah Mehmaan Sahi
Ohday Karbal Day Mosam Tai-En, Sajaad Koo Aan Bhira Kar.

5) Hovay Mojza Pak Shabab Honda, Qainath Wich Pak Quran Wangoo
Oda Jigar Namaz Jaya Hoya, Oda Kad Hovay Azan Wangoo
Oda Toar Day Baray Ai Khaliq, Mendi Bhain Day Naal Sila Kar.
