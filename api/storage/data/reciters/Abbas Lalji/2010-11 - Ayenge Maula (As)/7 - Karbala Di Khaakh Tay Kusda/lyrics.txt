**_Karbala Di Khaakh Tay Kusda_** ****

Karbala di khaakh tay kusda raiya Shabbir Zainab takdi raye.

Ummat neh maar chooreya bachra Batool da (x2) bayjurm tay baytak seer Zainab
takdi raye.

1)  Eh vekh tay veer edha koyi na raiya  
     Kalma go zulam kamonday ray  
     Koyi tapti howay rayt nu zakhmaan tay pahwaanda  
     Koyi pathar maray koyi teer Zainab takdi raye.

2)  Asghar di ambri murr murr kay  
     Peengay di dor halaandi ay  
     Khaimay day darr tay aan kay vahjaan vi maardi  
     Gaiya paani pheen sagheer Zainab takdi raye.

3)  Kadi honsala daivay Laila nu  
     Kadi aan Rubab nu gaal laavay  
     Kuj vi na kaar saaki jado ummat neh kaar laiyaan  
     Bharjaiyaan aan aseer Zainab takdi raye.

4)  Sir nangeyaan vekh kay phupiyaan nu  
     Sajaad bazaar ich rat roya  
     Abid di kandh tay zulam nu keetay layeen nay  
     Ona zulmaan di tehreer Zainab takdi raye.

5)  Haye ladli Faateh-e-Khaibar di  
     Rab janay kyun majboor hoyi  
     Maktal jo turr payi jado Ghazi de maarn tay  
     Looti jad chadar Tathheer Zainab takdi raye.
