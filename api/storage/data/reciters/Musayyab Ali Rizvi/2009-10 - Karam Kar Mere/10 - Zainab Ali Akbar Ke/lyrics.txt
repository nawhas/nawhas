**_Zainab Ali Akbar Ke jeenay ki dua mango_**

_ _

1: Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango

Kaha umm-e-laila ne basudd Ilteja mango



2: maan ne raat bhar apnay laal ki balaaen lin

beth kar musallay par jeenay ki duaen kin

mujh se pehlay ae beta mat apni qaza mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango



3: ae laal abhi tum ko main doolah banaongi

phoolon ka haseen sehra matahy pe sajaongi

baba se na tum jaldi marnay ki raza mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango



4: ghoray se gira joon hi chaand umm-e-laila ka

bibiyon k halkay main maan ne ye kiya noha

bach jae mera bacha milkar ye dua mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango



5: akbar ki sada sun kar khaimay se chalay sarwar

kehtay thay nazar kuch bhi ata nahi ae dilbar

main tum tak pohanch jaon bus itni dua mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango



6: zainab ne kaha bhaya main madad ko ajaon

saat main teray milkar laash-e-jawan uth waon

kaha sheh ne ae behna rub se hosla mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango



7: maatmi hay dunya main jo bhi ali akbar ka

sajtay hue maan daikhay uss k matahy par sehra

ye dua bhi ahel-e-aza sub behr-e-khuda mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango



8: ae sohail akbar ka jub kabhi pharo noha

akbar k waseelay se haat uthao pesh-e-khuda

lehja-e-jawan mango ghum-e-karbala mango

Zainab ali akbar k jeenay ki dua mango
