1: Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye

Main hogai b-e-aasra baba meray ajaeye



2: kanon se behta hay lahoo

shimr-e-laeen hay roo baroo

karti hon main aho buka

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye



3: ashoor se jagi hon main

baba bohat piyasi hon main

deti hon apna wasta

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye



4: mujh ko tamachay maar kar

cheenay gae meray gohar

main bhi hue hon be rida

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye



5: run ki fiza khamosh hay

our alkama khamosh hay

atay nahi meray cha cha

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye



6: mujh ko khuda se maang kar

bandha hay khud rakht-e-safar

kia iss liye ki thi dua

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye

7: bin ap k mar jaongi

phir ap ko yad aongi

apnay se mat kejay juda

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye



8: koofay main hum lae gae

phattar bhi barsae gae

barbaad kunba hogaya

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye



9: haae musayyab our zafar

tum sham main jao agar

aae gi zindaan se sada

baba meray ajaeye

Boli sakina ghum zada, baba meray ajaeye
