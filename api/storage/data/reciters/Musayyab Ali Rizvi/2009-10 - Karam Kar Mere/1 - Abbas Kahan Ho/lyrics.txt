1: Zainab ne kaha ye ro ro kar

bazaar main hon main nangay sir

abbas kahan ho ajao



2: main bekus hon dukh paai hon

main sarwar ki maan jaai hon

marjaongi ghum se ghut ghut kar

abbas kahan ho ajao



3: tum roothay akbar qatal hue

sub mooniso yawar qatal hue

be chaadar phirti hon dar dar

abbas kahan ho ajao



4: ye kesi musibat aae hay

har simt faqat tanhai hay

lillaha sahara do aakar

abbas kahan ho ajao



5: faryad hay ibn-e-shah-e-najaf

bazaar sajay hain charon taraf

ankhain hain guhar phat ta hay jigar

abbas kahan ho ajao



6: jana hay sar-e-darbar-e-jafa

abbas mujhe ati hay haya

maangu main bhala kis se chadar

abbas kahan ho ajao



7: qasim bhi nahi akbar bhi nahi

hud ye hay ali asghar bhi nahi

faryaad karon kis se jakar

abbas kahan ho ajao



8: zindaan main kabhi darbaron main

hay al-e-nabi bazaron main

emdaad karo ibn-e-haider

abbas kahan ho ajao



9: sub ahel-e-aza hain girya kunaan

hay aaj musayyab noha kunaan

kehta hay bilaal-e-khasta jigar

abbas kahan ho ajao
