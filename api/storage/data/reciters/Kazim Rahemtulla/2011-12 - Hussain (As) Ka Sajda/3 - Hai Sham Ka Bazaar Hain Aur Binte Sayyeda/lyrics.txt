Ya Hussain, Hussain (Ya Hussein (x7)

Haye Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda (x2)

_ _

1.Neze ki ek nok par dekhaga gaya sar-e-sarwar,

Ik simt se buland huwi binte batul ki chadar,

Ik simt alamdar hai aur binte Sayeda.

_Haye Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda_

2\. Zainab ki iss gharibi par rote hain haaye behrobar,

Shehzadi-e-najaf ke hi sarpar nahi rahi chadar,

Har sher-e-azadar hain aur binte Sayeeda.

_Haye Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda_

3\. Ghar se to ayithi Zainab ik qafele ko sung lekar,

Bazaar tak jo pohchni to, bhayi bhi na raha na pissar,

Baas abid-e-bimar hain aur binte Sayeeda.

_Haye Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda_

4\. Ik paal me ghar ujargaya, khayme jaale chine ghohar

Kya kya sitam huwe inpar, qurta jala diya kyunkar

Massuma ke rukhsar hain, aur binte sayeeda

_Haye Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda_

5\. Ghardan me tok bande hain, pawun me beriyaan logo

Saar par hain asmaan fakat, hathon me rassiyaan logo

Ankhen bhi ashk baar hain aur binte sayeeda

_Haye_ _ Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda_

6\. Haste huwe tamashayi aale nabi hain be chadar

Logo ka ek hajoom hain, Zainab khari hain nange saar

Tanhayi ki talwar hain aur binte sayeeda

_Haye_ _ Shaam ka Bazar hain aur binte Sayyeda_

7\. Ghash khagaya kalaam Kazim, kirtaas bhi hain nohaghar

Irfan me ne yeh missra jis daam utara khagaz par

Ab samne darbar hain aur binte sayadda

Haye Shaam ka Bazar hain (aur binte Sayyeda) (x3)
