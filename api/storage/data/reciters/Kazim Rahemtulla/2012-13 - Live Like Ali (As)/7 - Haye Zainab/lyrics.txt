Apne Bhai Ko Wo Rone Wali, Aj Qaidi Huwi Zainab (X2)
Chinnli Sar Se Bhi Chadar Uski, Zulm Sehti Rahe Zainab

1.   Dekho Ye Zulm-O-Sitam Ki Dunya, Koyi Hum Raaha Nahi Hai,
Na Mera Bhai Hai Na Ghazi Hai, Roke Kehti Rahi Zainab

2.   Kya Aziyat Hai Ye Bibi Tumpe, Durre Deta Tha Sitamgar,
Koyi Sunta Na Fughan Bibi Ki, Bas Tarapti Rahi Zainab

3.   Bekhata Qaidi Bani Bibi Yeh, Aur Haaton Me Rasan Hai,
Koyi Khata Na Taras Dukhya Par, Durre Khati Rahi Zainab

4.   Jab Sitamgar Ko Wo Kehti Thi Ke, Mera Bhai Bekhata Hai,
Aur Kat Ta Tha Gala Sarwar Ka, Khoon Roti Rahi Zainab

5.   Berida Hai Yeh Ali Ki Beti, Aur Hain Zehra Ki Jaayi
Karbala Se Shaam Qaidi Bankar, Haaye Chalti Rahi Zainab

6.   Berida Kufay Ki Galiyon Me Jab, Haaye Zainab Ko Phiraye,
Aansun Ka Darya Bahakar Itna, Ashk Peeti Rahi Zainab

7.   Khutba Zainab Ne Diya Aisa Ke, Huwa Khamosh Sitamgar,
Bint-E-Haider Hun Me Bint-E-Zehra, Roke Kehti Rahi Zainab

8.   Mera Kehta Hai Qalam Akbar Yeh, Bargah Zainab Me Roke,
Sunke Kazim Dard Ka Nauha Yeh, Haaye Roti Rahi Zainab
