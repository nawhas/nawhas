Ya Hussein (X2) Ya Ghareeb, Ya Shaheed (X2) ((X2))

Chehlum Hai Azadaro Ye Shabbir Ka Chehlum Hai (X2)
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jagir Ka Chehlum Hai (X2)

1.  Beparda Hai Zainab To Wo Roti Hai Bibiyaan,
Ab Kaun Sahara De Ye Dukhiya Rahe Tanha,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

2.  Zainab Ko Aaj Koyi Nahi Dekhne Wala,
Apna Yeh Gham Sunati Hai Roroke Ye Dukhiya,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

3.  Ghar Lootliya Khayme Jale Durre Bhi Lagaye,
Ghar Lootke Saidaniyou Ko Qaidi Banaye,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

4.  Bhai Ki Wo Turbat Pe Bahati Hai Jo Aansu,
Zainab Pe Jo Guzri Thi Roroke Kya Kahun
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

5.  Zainab Ne Sunae Hain Ye Sitam Baad-E-Karbala,
Khaimon Ko Jalayagaya Zainab Ko Rulaya,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

6.  Zainab Ne Sunayi Wo Sakina Ki Kahani,
Sarwar Ke Baad Sham-E-Ghariba Chali Ayi,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

7.  Qasim Nahi Akbar Nahi Asghar Bhi Nahi Hai,
Abbas Sa Bhai Tera Lashkar Bhi Nahi Hai,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai

8.  Bekas Ki Ziarat Ko Chale Kazim-O-Akbar,
Sunkar Yeh Jo Nauha Tuje Bulwate Hai Sarwar,
Zehra Ke Lute Ghar Ki Wo Jageer Ka Chehlum Hai
