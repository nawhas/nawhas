Ya Imam-e-zamaan (x8)

Hai ye lab per dua ya imam-e-Zamaan, (x2)

Ya Imam-e-Zaman (x4)

1)  Hai ye ahle aza ki dua ya imam, (x2)

    Ho zahur-e-imam-e-zaman ab tamam, (x2)

     Hai ye dil ki sada, ya imam-e-zamaan

2)  Aengay ap to hoga ab faisla, (x2)

    Mitjaengay dunya se zulm-o-jafa, (x2)

       Baar-way murtuza,ya imam-e-zamaan



 __



3)  Wast-e-shere Haider ka dete hai hum, (x2)

    Wasta maula sarwar ka dete hai hum, (x2)

       Dikhlado mojiza ya imam-e-zamaan



 __



4)  Kaun bhulega karbal ke rudad-e-gham, (x2)

    Bhulegi na ye duniya wo saray sitam, (x2)

     Kaisi thi woh jafa,ya imam-e-zamaan



 __



5)  Ap ke aane se sab badal jayega, (x2)

    Duniya ko sacha rasta ye miljaaega, (x2)

       Dekhega ye jahan, imam-e-zamaan



 __



6)  Aap ke aanay se hoga roshan samaan (x2)

    Saaye me aapke hoga sara jahan (x2)

      Khush hai ahle-aza,ya imam-e-zamaan



 __



7)  Hai ye Kazim ke lab per dua ya imam, (x2)

    Aur Akbar ne dil se kaha hai kalaam, (x2)

       Hum ko dedo jazaa,ya imam-e-zamaan
