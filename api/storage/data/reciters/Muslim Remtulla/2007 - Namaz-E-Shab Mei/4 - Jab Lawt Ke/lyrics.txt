  1. Jab Lawt ke Karbala se, asire sitam chale

Sajjad sar barehna, badardo alam chale

Rote saroko pit te, paabande gham chale

Zainab ne lash-e-sheh se, kahaa bhai ham chale

Marne se aapke mai, izaa ye uthaati hu,

Darbar me Yazid ke, sar nange jaati hu

  2. Bhai tumhari doori se marjaaengi bahan,

Kyun kar tumhaari laashko, dafnaengi bahan

Kaahe ko chut ke qaidse, ab aengi bahan

Kistarha aah aapko, ab paegi bahan

Naachar qaid zulm me, wo khastatan chali,

Lo bhai shehre Shaam ki, jaanib bahan chali

  3. Sompa tujhe khudaa ko, ay Zehra gulozaar

Is teri bekasi ko, bahan ko, bahan hoogayi nisaar

Hai hai ye ret gharm me, Gulshan badan figaar

Ye halq nazneen teraa, ay tege asdaar

Gardan kataake bhaayi, bayabaan me sogaye,

Ghafeel bahan se aan, ke jangal me sogaye

  4. Hai hai mere ghareeb, mere mahalaka Hussain,

Hai hai mere musafire, karbobala Hussain

Hai hai tujhe na paanika katra mila Hussain

Hai hai tamaama tan tere, tukre huwa Hussain

Pyaase galepe khanjare, bedaad chalgaya
