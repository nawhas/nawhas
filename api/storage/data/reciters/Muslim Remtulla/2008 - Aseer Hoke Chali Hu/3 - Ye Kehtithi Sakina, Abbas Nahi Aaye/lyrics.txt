1. Ye Kehtithi Sakina, Abbas Nahi Aaye,

Pani Ke Liye Teri, Abbas Nahi Aaye,

  2. Dekhaake Alam Aaya, Ummeed Thi Ye Dil Me,

Pilwaungi In Sabko, Abbas Nahi Aaye,

  3. Baaba Ki Kamar Tuti, Kya Wajha Ye Na Samji,

Dekhaake Alam Aaya, Abbas Nahi Aaye,

  4. Shaanose Judaa Aaye, Do Haat Ye Tumhare,

Ranme Pare Hai Mushk Ko, Abbas Nahi Aaye,

  5. Paani Ki Pilane Ka, Tumhe Kiya Tha Waada,

Wo Ho Na Saka Tum Se, Abbas Nahi Aaye,

  6. Tha Sabki Dilme Paani, Khayme Me Jo Ayega,

Pilwawungi Bacho Ko, Abbas Nahi Aaye

  7. Roro Ke Puchtithi, Baali Sakina Sab Se,

Baba To Alam Laaya, Abbas Nahi Aaye

  8. Dekho Chamak Rahaa Hai, Panjaa Alam Ka Hay Hay,

Kis Wajha Se Ay Baba, Abbas Nahi Aaye

  9. Phir Nane Hath Utaakar, Baali Sakina Boli,

Ya Rub Tu Juld Madad Kar, Abbas Nahi Aaye
