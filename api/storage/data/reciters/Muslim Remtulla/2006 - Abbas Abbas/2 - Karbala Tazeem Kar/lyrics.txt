Kerbala Taazim Kar, Aai Bahan Shabbir Ki

Jaake Kadmose Lipat, Tu Zainab-E-Dilgeer Ki.

  1. Kabr Se Be Sar Bahattar, Tan Uthe Taazim Ko

Dekh Istekbaal Ko Hay, Furaat-E-Shamshir Ki

  2. Kaidse Chhutker Hai Aata, Kaafela Zawwar Ka,

Puchhle Unse Sitam, Zindaan Ki Jaagir Ka

  3. Kaale Mehmil Me Hai Baithi, Bibiya Sab Dukh Jali

Daagose Chhalni Jigar Hai, Shaah Ki Hamshir Ki

  4. Nannisi Turbat Ye Asghar, Ki Chupaale Kerbala,

Kya Tarapna Soon Sakoge, Maadare Beshir Ka

  5. Maadare Akber Bhi Aati, Hai Kaleja Thaam Kar,

Wo Gilaa Kisse Kare, Ruthi Hui Takdir Ki

  6. Tum Sakina Ko Na Dhundho, Turbate Abbas Par,

Reh Gayi Hai Shaam Me, Wo Laadli Shabbir Ki
