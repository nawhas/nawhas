Aabad hui Kerbobala, ujra Madina, barbaad hu mei

Yu hazrate Zainabe kahaa pit ke sinaa, barbaad hui mei.

  1. Ek din tha ke chaader mere sarse giri thi, suraj bhi na nikla,

Sar nange firaata hai mujhe aaj kamina, barbaad hui mei.

  2. Ek din tha ke is shehr ki karthithi mei shaahi, aur aaj tabaahi,

Be ghusl paraa mohre nabuwwat ka nagina, barbaad hui mei.

  3. Bhai muwe bete muwe, zindaame fase hai, waaris nahin koyi,

Abbas nahi jite shaahe Madina, barbaad hui mei.

  4. Ye nil tamaachoke mere muhpe pare hai, mei kisko dikhawu,

Nezeke tale beithke roti hai Sakina, barbaad hui hai

  5. Kaano ko mere chirke dur legaya zaalim, mei rehgayi roti,

Ye chaandsa chehra nahi dekhegi Sakina, barbaad hui mei

  6. Kaasim nahi Akber nahi Abbas nahi hai, Aabid hai to bimaar,

Yeh kaisa paraa mujhpe Muharram ka mahina, barbaad hui mei.

Aabad hui Kerbobala, ujra Madina, barbaad hu mei
