Kerbalki Kahaani Jab, Koi Bhi Sunayi Ga

Dil Walo Ke Ankhose. Ek Darya Bahaayega.

  1. Wo Karwaan Sarwarka, Jo Shaan Se Nikla Tha

Sughra Ko Khabar Kya Thi, Na Lot Ke Aayega.

  2. Mehmaan Bulaya Hai, Ay Kufiyo Sarwarko

Kya Kumba Shahe Deen Ka, Paani Nahi Payega.

  3. Paamal Huwa Kaasim, Yu Goro Ki Taapose

Kis Tarha Chacha Teri, Mayyat Ko Uthayenga.

  4. Tha Hathoke Katneka, Kuch Gham Na Dilawarko

Saydaniyo Ka Parda, Ab Kon Bachayega

  5. Tooti Hai Kamar Shehki, Ghabrana Magar Akber

Farzande Nabi Teri, Laashe Ko Uthayenga.

  6. Sote Na The Tum Asghar, Jule Me Tarapte The

Dunya Ko Khabar Kya Thi, Ek Teer Sulaayenga.

  7. Ashaab Ne Dam Tora, Shabbir Ke Zanu Par

Kis Zaanu Par Sar Apna, Shabbir Kataayenga

  8. Sarwarne Uthaethe, Sab Saathiyoke Laashe

Shabbir Tera Laasha, Ab Kon Uthayenga
