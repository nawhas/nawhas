Mere Mawla mujhe do sahaara, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara

Dhalte suraj ko tumne ubhaara, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.

  1. Tumhi Mushkil Kushaa do jaaha ho, Tumhi Mawla-e-kawno makaa ho, Ambiyaane bhi tumko pukaaraa, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
  2. Meri Kashti bhawarme fasi hay, Dushmanoke labo par hasi hai, Kardo nazdeek mujhse kinaaraa, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
  3. Gardishome mukaddar hai mera, Kya karu chaarsu hai andhera, Tum jo chaaho to chamke sitaara, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
  4. Ranj me kabse mei mubtilaahu, Kabse Mawla aseere balaa hu, Kya tarapna hai meraa ghawaaraa, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
  5. Munharif ho chala hai zamaana, Zindagi ban rahi hai fasaana, Dekho meri taraf ab khudaraa, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
  6. Mustafa ke liye ab bachaalo, Fatema ke liye jaan churaawo, Ab to mutlak nahi gham ka saayaa, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
  7. Waasta Akbare nawjawaanka, Waasta chhe mahine ki jaan ka, Kardo rahmat ka bas ab isharaa, Mayne thaama hai daaman tumhara.
