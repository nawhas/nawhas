  2. Illahi Tujhe Wasta Panjatanka, Ho Shaadaab Guncha Dilonke Chamanka, Ye Daaman Hamara Muradose Se Bhardo, Karam Ki Nazarse Dua Maangta Hu

  3. Jo Muflis Hai Unko, To Dawlat Ata Kar, Jo Bimar Hai Unko Sehat Atakar, Gharibo Pe Tu Rehm Kar Ya Illahi, Marizo Khatir Shafaa Maangta Hu

  4. Illahi Hame Shado Masrur Rakhna, Pareshaniya Hamse Sada Duur Rakhna, Pareshaniya Hamko Gheri Huwi Hai, Pareshaniyo Ki Dua Maangtaa Hu

  5. Huwa Hai Na Maayus Tere Sa Wali, Nahi Tere Dharse Fira Koi Khaali, Yehi Iltija Ek Mere Hi Khatir, Mey Sabke Liye Muddua Maangta Hu
