Isteghasa Hai "Rehan Azmi" Mera

Lashkar-E-Imam(A.s.) Mein Hon Saaray Matmi

"Farhan" Majlis-E-Hussain(A.s.) Mein Karay Dua

(Aeay Maula Aeay Maula, Lashkar-E-Hussain Ban Kay Aeay Maula) X2

  1. Gharian Intezar Ki Tamam Ho Chukin

Zulm-O-Jor Ki Fizaen Aam Ho Chukin

Aap(A.s.) Kay Zahoor Ki Jo Thin Nishanian

Roonuma Woh Sab Har Aik Gaam Ho Chukin

Dair Ho Chuki Sahib-Uz-Zamaan(A.s.)

Lashkar-E-Hussain(A.s.) Ban Kay Aeay Maula

  2. De Rahay Hain Kab Say Al-Ajal Ki Hum Sada

Bhaijtay Hain Har Baras Areezay Aur Dua

Mauj Ko Banalia Hai Apna Namah Bar

Jaanti Hai Mauj Hi To Aap(A.s.) Ka Pata

Aapp(A.s.) Kay Liey Hum Tarhaptay Hain

Lashkar-E-Hussain(A.s.) Ban Kay Aeay Maula

  3. Masjidein Pukarti Hain Har Namaz Mein

Bargahein Dhondti Hain Har Ghari Tumhain

Majlison Mein Parhtay Hain Dua-E-Nahia

Aarzoo Hai Is Sadi Mein Tum Ko Daikhlein

Daur Khatm Ho Ab Yeh Ghaib Ka

Lashkar-E-Hussain(A.s.) Ban Kay Aeay Maula

(Aeay Maula Aeay Maula, Lashkar-E-Hussain Ban Kay Aeay Maula) X2

  4. Intezar-E-Adl Hai Adalaton Ko Bhi

Aap(A.s.) Aakhri Imam(A.s.) Barhwein(12Th) Ali(A.s.)

Aap(A.s.) Aeay To Dil Ko Chain Aaeyga

Aap(A.s.) Hi Kay Intezar Mein Hai Zindagi

Taigh-E-Haidery(A.s.) Aap(A.s.) Laengay

  5. Hain Libas-E-Deen Mein Mukhalif-E-Aza

Baichnay Lagay Khateeb Zikr-E-Karbala

Para Para Kardia Hai Ittehad Ko

Majlison Say Hai Namaz Ka Muqabla

Waris(A.s.)-E-Aza Deer Na Laga

Lashkar-E-Hussain(A.s.) Ban Kay Aeay Maula

  6. Parhtay Hain Namaz Sheh(A.s.) Kay Saaray Matmi

Zikr Hai Namaz Ka To Majlison Mein Bhi

Sajda-E-Hussain(A.s.) Ka Jisay Khayal Hai

Janta Hai Manzilat Ko Woh Namaz Ki

Is Tafarraqay Ko Mitaeay

  7. Paash Paash Aap(A.s.) Ka Bhi Dil Hai Ya Imam(A.s.)

Jal Rahay Thay Jis Gharhi Hussain(A.s.) Kay Khayam

  8. Ris Raha Hai Khoon Ab Bhi Jis Kay Kaan Say
