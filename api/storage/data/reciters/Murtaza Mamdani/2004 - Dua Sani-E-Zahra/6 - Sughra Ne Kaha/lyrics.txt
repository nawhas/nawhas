Sughra ne kaha

**_

(Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chorke jaate ho) x2

  1. beemar bahan ko kiun ay bhai rulatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

  2. tanhai main ghut ghut kay kay marjaongi ay bhai

beemar pay hoti hai (ik marg ya tanhai) x2

jeetay ji mairi turbat kiun ghar main banatay ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

  3. jatay ho chalay jao itna to bata daitay

tum bin ye behan tanha (ji paayegi ab kaisay) x2

aankhon main jo aansu hain kiun mujh say chopatay ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

  4. Pardes me ay bhaayi shaadi na rachaalena

Sehra na sajaalena (kangwaa na nahi kangna) x2

Tum saath me shaadi ka choraey le jaate ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

  5. Kya baat hai dil mera reh rehke dharakta hai

Aankhon mei meri har dam (neza sa chamakta hai) x2

Hai baat koi aisi jo mujhse chpaate ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

  6. rehan likhon kaisay sughra ki wo faryadain

jab khon bahati thi (anson ki jaga aankhain) x2

ro ro kay wo kahti thi kiun choar kay jatay ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

  7. beemar bahan ko kiun ay bhai rulatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho

Sughra nay kaha bhayya kyun choar kay jatay ho, kyu chor ke jaate ho
