Bint-E-Nabi Ke Dil Ki Dua Matam-E-Hussan

Har Dhor Har Sadi Ki Sada Matam-E-Hussain



1.     Fikr-E-Yazeediat Ne Boht Ki Hai Koshisheh

Lekin Kahan Kissi Se Ruka Matam-E-Hussain



2.     Dolat Ye Sirf Sheh Ke Azadaar Ko Milli

Nad-E-Ali Hadis Kissa Matam-E-Hussain



3.     Har Haath Ko Naseeb Kahan Ye Hunar Hua

Hai Khass Fatima Ki Ata Matam-E-Hussain



4.     Batla Rahi Hai Shaam Ke Bazar Ki Fizza

Karta Hai Inqalab Bapa Matam-E-Hussain

5.     Mange Thi Jis Leyin Ne Bayat Hussain Se

Zainab Ne Usske Ghar Mai Kia Matam-E-Hussain



6.     Uss Ka Koi Yazeed Se Rishta Zaroor Hai

Jis Ko Bhi Lag Ra Hai Bura Matam-E-Hussain



7.     Ehl-E-Haram Jo Aae Madine Mai Lout Ke

Roze Pe Mustafa Ke Hua Matam-E-Hussain



8.     Jis Ke Kate Hain Baazu Taakulum Furaat Pe

Kismaat Mei Meri Uss Ne Lihka  Matam-E-Hussain(A.s)
