SHAAM HAEY SHAAM

** **

1.     NAAM ZAINAB HAI MERA MEIN HOON HAAYA KI TASVEER

GUM KI CHADER HAI MEREY SAR PEY HUWI AAJ ASEER

SABAR-E-SHABBIR ATTA KARDEY MUJHEY MEREY KHUDA

BEY RIDA MEIN HOON MEREY SAATH ZAMANEY KA AMEER



2.     MEIN NEY AKBAR KA KALEJA JO NIKALTEY DEKHA

KHOON ASGHAR KO RUKH-E- SHAH PEY MALTEY DEKHA

TERI BETI HOON JO ZINDA HOON ABHI TAK BABA

MEIN NEY MAQTAL MEIN GALLA SHEH KA BHI KATTEY DEKHA



3.     TOUQ O ZANJEER MEIN JAKRA HAI BADAN SAABIR KA

SAR SINA PER HAI KHUDA DEKH TEREY ZAKIR KA

KITNI DUSHWAAR HAI MUJH PER YEAH QYAMAAT KI GHARI

PUSHT-E-ABID PEY BANDHA HAI JO GALLA BAQIR KA



4.     NAUQ-E-NAIZA PEY SAKINA KI NAZAR JAATI HAI

USS GHARI KHAUF SEY BEY SAAKHTA CHILLATI HAI

SAR-E-ABBAS NAZAR AATA NAHI HAI USKO

HAIY PABAND-E-RASAN KHAK PAY GIR JATI HAY



5.     SHAAM WALON SEY RIDA MANGTI REHJAOONGHI

MAJMA-E-AAM HAI ABBAS MEIN MARJAUNGHI

NAUK-E_NAIZA SEY TEREY SAR KO JO GIRTEY DEKHA

TEREE GAIRAT KEY TASAVUR MEIN GUZAR JAOOUNGHI



6.     AGAEE SHAAM KEY BAZAAR MEIN BINT-E-HAIDER

NEEL SHANOON PEY LIYEAH SAATH HAI SHEH KI DUKHTER

KAISEY YAWAR MEIN LIKHOON GHUM SEY JIGAAR PHATTA HAI

LAB PEY SAJJAAD KEY EIK LAFZ THA CHADAR CHADAR
