Note For Reciters : This Nauha Contains "Doray". Doray (Tehtay Lafz) Are
Words Not Recited In A Tone, During The Course Of Recitation. Doray Are
Usually Recited During Processions Or Where There Is A High Response (Matam)
From The People. It Would Be Advisable To Avoid During Normal Mehfil
Recitations. These Are Marked With "\------".
