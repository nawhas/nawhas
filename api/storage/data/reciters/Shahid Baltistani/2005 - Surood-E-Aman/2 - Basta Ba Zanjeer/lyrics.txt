Jumla Yateema Haay

  1. Jaltay Huwe Ghar Lutti Ridaye(N) Ah Hussaina

Kisko Sadaa De Kisko Bulaye Ah Hussaina. Haay

(Ah Hussaina Shahe Shaheeda, Bastaba Zanjeer Jumla Yateema) X2

  2. Eik Zanjeer-E-Sitam Bara Aseero Kay Gale

Us Pay Ye Hukm Kay Har Peer-O- Jawaan Tez Chalay

Ee Sitam Haay Hussaina

Pusht Pay Partay Rahe Saath Durron Kay Nishaan Haay

(Ah Hussaina Shahe Shaheeda, Bastaba Zanjeer Jumla Yateema) X2

  3. Eik Beemar Aseeron Ka Jo Salaar Bana

(Ah Hussaina Shahe Shaheeda, Bastaba Zanjeer Jumla Yateema) X2

  4. Dhoop Say Pyar Hay Saaye Se Sarokar Nahi

Koi Baanu Ki Tarha Bekaso Ghamkhwar Nahi

(Ah Hussaina Shahe Shaheeda, Bastaba Zanjeer Jumla Yateema) X2

  5. Umm-E-Laila Ka Jigar Hogaya Paara Paara

(Ah Hussaina Shahe Shaheeda, Bastaba Zanjeer Jumla Yateema) X2

  6. Eik Nawshah Ki Mayat Ke Bahattar Tukre

  7. Wo Jo Lashkar Ka Alamdar Tha Dast-E-Shabbir

  8. Kaan Zakhmi Hai Gala Khushk Jalaa Hay Daman

  9. Be Ridayi Ki Aziyat Ko Uthane Waale
