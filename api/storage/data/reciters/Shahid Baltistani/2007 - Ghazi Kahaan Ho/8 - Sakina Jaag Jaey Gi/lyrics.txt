Majawo shor na logo sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  1. Kabhi Bachi ko takta hai kabhi aansoo(n) bahata hai

Dar-e-Zindan se behta musafir sabse kehtaa hai

zaraa aaram se guzro sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  2. Kisi bhi Zulm ke jo aah ki khatir wo lab khulte

Tarap ker Abid-e-bimar ye kehta thaa hoto se

agar ye aah nikli to sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  3. Agar Aulaad wale ho tumhe ye sab pataa hoga

adhoori neendh se uth jaye jo bachi to kiya hoga

khudara shimr ko roko sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  4. Abhi Zindan me hojayi ga kohraam ye barpa

who aahat simt ke samjegi ke shayed aa gaye baba

Dar-e-Zindan naa kholo sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  5. Ye wadaa hai meraa pani nahi mangooga me tum se

wo jab uthtjaya to jee bhar ke beshak maar na dure

abhi bekas ko na maro sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  6. Abhi azad ho tum qayd ke matlab ko kiya jano

parindo chordo zindan meri iltejaa maango

chale jawoo naa yu chehko sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  7. Sar-e-Zinda sakina ko kazaa ki neend kiyaa aayi

kisi se kuch nahi kehtaa hai itna mazloom thaa bhayi

ke ab ye der nahi usko sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  8. Agar Aulaad wale ho tumhe ye sab pataa hoga

adhoori neendh se uth jaye jo bachi to kiya hoga

khudara shimr ko roko sakina Jaag Jayengi

(Meri Zanjeer Na Khencho sakina Jaag Jayengi) x2

  9. Saf-e-matam se abid ka ye "Akbar" bayn aata hai

Isse bas waaste purse meri ab cheyn aata hai

Sada matam ki na roko, Sakina jaag jayengi
