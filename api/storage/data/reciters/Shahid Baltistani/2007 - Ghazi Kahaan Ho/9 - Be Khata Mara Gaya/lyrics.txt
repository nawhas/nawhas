(Ya Hassan) X6

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan-E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X3

  1. Sehr Se Nikle Jigar, Khu(N) Se Kafan Laal Huwa

Wa Hassan-E-Sabz-E-Qaba (Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan
-E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2

  2. Oogal Te Huwe Bhayi Ko Behan Ne Dekha (Or Tarap Ker Ye Kaha) X2 Teri Gurbat Pe Bhayi Teri Zainab Ho Fidaa Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2

  3. Maa Ke Dukh Se Abhi Azad Kaha(N) Thi Zainab (Hogaya Kaisa Gazab) X2 Baap Zinda Na Raha Bhayi Suwe Khuld Gaya Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2

  4. Ker Diya Zahr-E-Hala Hal Ne Jigar Ke Tukre (Aur Qasim Ke Liye) X2 Kamsini Me Hai Ye Gham Sham-E-Gharibi Jaisa Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2

  5. Aise Ik Baar Janaza Jo Nikal Jata Hai (Jab Wo Phir Aata Hain) X2 Aisa Taboot Hai Ye Lot Ke Jo Ghar Ayaa Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2

  6. Sheh Ne Us Wakht Jo Ghazi Ko Na Roka Hoga (Hashr Ho Jata Bapaa) X2 Teer Aate Na Janaze Pe Tumhare Maula Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2

  7. Sheh Ko "Rehan" Baraadar Kaa Jigar Yaad Aya (Jab Se Karbobala) X2 Tukde Qassim Ke Utha Te Huwe Maula Ne Kaha Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba

(Be Khata Mara Gaya Sibte Rasool-E-Dosara Wa Hassan -E-Sabaz-E-Qaba) X2
