1. Haae sakina, qaid hokar, shaam ke zindaan mei, jab aayi

Woh bachi, us andhere, ghar ki taariki se ghabraayi

Zamin to farsh thi saaya faghan tha, charkh neel aayi

Muqaddar mei musibat ki ye pehli raat dikhlaayi

  2. Phuphi ke paas rehti thi, naa maa ke paas soti thi

Haae phuphi ke paas rehti thi, naa maa ke paas soti thi

Haae barehnaa sar khari zindaan ke darwaaze pe roti thi

  3. Udhar se, jo guzar ta tha use kehti thi sultaan jaa

Mei bekas qaid mei baithi hun ik paigaam hai mera

Agar baba mile tujko to tu kehnaa qasam khaata

Sakina par musibat hai khabar lo ai shahe waala

  4. Agar tujse kahen khaimon mei sota chor aaya hun

To tu kehna dare zindaan pe rota chor aaya hun

Sakina
