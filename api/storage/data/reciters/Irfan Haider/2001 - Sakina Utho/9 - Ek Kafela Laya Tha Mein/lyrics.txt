Ek kafela laya tha mein

**_

Sula hai kabr mai besheer ko husaini ghareeb,

Har ek shaheed ke lashe pe aaye hai rote huwe,

Labe husain se suye falak aayi sadaa

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Us kafele mein tha ali akber sa ek beta jawaan,

Abbas jesa bhayi tha,

(Masum asgher be zubaan) x2

Kuch bibiya thi saath mein mehshar mein jinka naam tha

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Ye kafela kaise luta ye soch ke mai ro diya,

Pala tha jinko naaz se,

(karbobala mein kho diya),

Un sab ke laashe samne the aur hoto par buka

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Karyal jawaan ki laash hatho par uthayi hai abhi,

Ek be zuban ki kabr bhi,

(Maine banayi hai abhi) x2

Samaan mere katl ka karte hai ab ye ashkiya

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Pyase mere atfaal hai behta hai darya samne,

Ek be watan ko dasht mein,

(Luta hai foje shaam ne) x2

Roti hai mere chah ne waalo ke laasho par kazaa

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Kismat meri laykar muje karbobala tak agayi,

Ab barte barte bat

(Zainab ki rida tak agayi) x2

Ho jawoonga mein katl to chinjayegi uski rida

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Kuch der mein kanjar laeen ka halk par chal jayenga,

Aur baad mere khaym-e

(Sadaat bhi jal jaayenga) x2

Ya yu koi pardes mein aa kar lootaye karbela

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

Jab katl mein ho jawoonga shaame ghariba ayengi,

(taaza kayamat dhayengi) x2

us waqt uthengi meri laashe buridaa se sadaa

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

lashe sabhi rehjayengi jalti zamin par bekafan,

kaydi banengi mustufa

(ki aal pabandhe rasan) x2

Raho mein rota jayenga bimar ye karke buka

(Ek kafela laya tha mein) x3

(saydaniya zindan mein) x2

sajaad mera kayd se jayenga ye kehte huwa.
