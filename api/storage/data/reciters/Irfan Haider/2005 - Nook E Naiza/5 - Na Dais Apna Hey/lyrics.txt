Karbala se chali eik bekas behen. Bhaayi se aalam-e-gurbat watan yaad aya. Sab
shaheedo ko kehti huwi alvida. Bhaayi ki laash pe pohnchi to kafan yaad aya.
Khud ko naake se giraakar Zainab kehne lagi roro kar. Na deys apna hai, na log
apne hai
