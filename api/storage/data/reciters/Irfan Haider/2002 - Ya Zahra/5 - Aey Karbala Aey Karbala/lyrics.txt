Ay Karbala X3

En Nara O Shevan En Girya O Zaari Dar Khaima-E-Shabbir Bapa Shaud

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  1. Jar Janib Hai Sitamgar (Be Khata Aal-E-Payambar) X2

Koi Be Makhna Aur Chadar (Koi Be Hosh Zamee Par) X2

Shud Zainab-E Girya Ba Haal-E-Pareshaa As Dast-E-Sitamgar Be Rida Shaud

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  2. Jal Uthao Aag Se Sehra (Kahi Khaima Kahi Jhoola) X2

Har Taraf Aik Hai Manzar (Kahi Matam Kahi Noha) X2
As Aatishe Shoza Aznaar-E-Farawa Paamaal-E-Sitam Aale Aba Shaud

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  3. Aya Yeh Kaisa Muharram (Bibiya Roti Hai Paigham) X2

Khoon Barsati Hai Aankhe (Khoon Me Duba Hai Parcham) X2

Aamacho Muharram Girya Khona Aalam Khoona Ba Fisha Chashme Aza Shaud

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  4. Hai Kahi Laasha-E-Akbar (To Kahi Turbat-E-Asghar) X2

Zakhm Kis Derja Lage Hai (Haye Bano Ke Jigar Par) X2

Ham Shakley Payambar Lakhte Dil-E-Sarwar Asteero Sina Asr-E-Kaza Shaud

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  5. Binte Zahra Ki Dukhaee (Saat Roti Hai Khudaee) X2

De Gaya Daag-E-Judaee (Haay Abbas Sa Bhai) X2

Be Taab Se Gham Khwaa Shut Sani-E-Zahra Abbas Labe Nehr Fiza Shaud

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  6. Aise Bikra Koi Lasha (Jaise Bikra Hua Sehra) X2

Khaak Par Bethi Hai Farwa (Aur Karti Hai Ye Noha) X2

Lakhte Dil-E-Farwa Gum Gashtare Sehra Paamaal-E-Sitam Noha Laka Shuad

(Ai Karbala Karbala Ai Karbala) X2

  7. Ik Taraf Pyaaso Ka Girya (Ik Taraf Rota Hai Darya) X2

Marsiya Parti Hai Fiza (Aur Kehti Hai Ye Zehra) X2

Gham Tashna Labara Darya-E-Rawara Intash Nalabe Kehro Bara Shaud
