So Gai Ashor Aur Bedar Hai Sham-E-Alam

Din Ki Har Sakhti Say Ab Duswar Hai Sham-E-Alam



1- Din Kay Qatal E Aam May Saray Muhafiz Kho Gaay

Bay Rida Zainab(As) Howi Khon Bar Hai Sham-E-Alam



2- Din Nay Deakha Hai Abu Talib Kay Beato Ka Jalal

Deakka Kya Kirdar Hai Sham-E-Alam



3- Sir To Bhai Day Chukay Bhaino Kay Parday Hai Abi

Tu Bata Ab Tuj Ko Kya Darkar Hai Sham-E-Alam



4- Muqtal E Shabbir May Bhi Jaa Nahi Sakti Bahain

Aj Zainab(As) Kay Liyea Dewaar Hai Sham-E-Alam



5- John Jab Chadar Chini Thi Tab Say Zainab(As) Kay Liyea

Dil Pay Ek Rakha Howa Angar Hai Sham-E-Alam
