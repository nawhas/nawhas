2- Wo Piyas Lay Kay Aya Maidan May Azmanay

Bay Deen Saray Kuu Kar Ansoo Lagay Bahanay

Talwar To Kisi Par, Baysheer Nay Chalaii Nai



3- Zindan Sham Wala Inj Pa Gaya Hai Doori

Rah Gaii Ay Sham Tainu Dain Dee Si Jairi Loori

4- Shabbir Kabar Wich Wi Asghar(As) Nu Naal Rakiya

5- Do Kabra Maa Nay Dhobtay Ay Soch Kay Banaya

Matay Aakay Ma Nu Asghar(As) Muiya Nah Judaya

Meri Bhain Naal Meri, Turbat Vi Maa Banai Nai
