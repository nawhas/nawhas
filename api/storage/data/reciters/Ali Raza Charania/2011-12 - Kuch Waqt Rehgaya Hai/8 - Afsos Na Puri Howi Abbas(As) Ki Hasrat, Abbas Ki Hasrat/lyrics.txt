1- bachpan say yahi baat suna karta tha ghazi

Hai shah e madina kay liyea jaan tumhari

2- wo waqt bhi aya to qayamat say na kam tha

Lashay pay hasan kay jo kiya tiro nay matam



3- jab nahar say khaimo ko hatanay pay pari baat

Zainab nay kiyea kabuu abbas(as) kay jazbat



4- ansaro nay bhi jang ki takbeer lagai

Laikin na raza jang ki abbas(as) nay paii



5- sir apna jukaay howa shabbir say bola

Ho jang ki muj ko bhi ijazat meray mola



6- jo dil ki thi hasrat wo rahi dil may hi dab kar

Mil pai na maqtal ki riza hay muqaddar



7- afsos kay bay dash e jari hogaya daim

Sahil kay kareeb shair e ali sogaya daim
