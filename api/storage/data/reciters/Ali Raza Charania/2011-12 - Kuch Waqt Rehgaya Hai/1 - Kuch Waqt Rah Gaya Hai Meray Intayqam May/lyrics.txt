Kuch waqt rah gaya hai meray intayqam may,
rahnay na donga ab mai sakina ko sham mai

1- bibi  hai teri baliya hatho mai meray aur,
gaibat khari howi hai daray iktaytam mai

2-kano say kho baha tera jis jis makam par,
mai roz deakta raha gaibat tamam mai

3-zindan mai wo teri lahad par bahaonga,
aso bacha howay hai jo ankho kay jam may

4-lagti hai piyas jab bi teray sath har ghari,
asghar(as) ko deakhta hu mai pani kay jam may

5- asghar ki kabar khodnay ka gham liyea
howay,hai zulfikar ro rahe ab tak nayam may

6- noha to yea hai john mai har pal hu deakta,
na mus apnay ghar ki khari is daham mai
