Bat to sin say siwa kar gaay, zainab(as) kay pisar
jaan mamu pay fida kar gaay, zainab(as) kay pisar



1- sin may chotay thay magar azam tha allah allah

Bhagnay ka kisi badnasal ko moqa na diya

Haq ghulami ka ada kar gaay, zainab(as) kay pisar



2- cha gaay sham kay laskar par ba andaz e ali

Yaad phir hamla e jaffar ki sabhi ko aii

Shan say wada wafa kar gaay, , zainab(as) kay pisar



3- jang e khaibar kay nazar anay lagay nazara

Sham ki fojo may dar aay ali kay pyaray

Hashar maidan may bapa kar gaay , zainab(as) kay pisar



4- aa kay maidan may khaimo ki taraf ruk na kiya

Alkam cheen kay mojo ki taraf ruk na kiya

Jo tha zainab(as) nay kaha kar gaay, , zainab(as) kay pisar



5- jurrat shair e khuda fauj ko dikhanay kay bad

Chand say sino pay sham shiro sina khanay kay bad

Khud ko mamu pay fida kar gaay , zainab(as) kay pisar



6- hai maidan e wiga ka wo sulagta manzar

Kab talak jang bhi kartay wo bhala tisna jigar

Hai piyasay hi qaza kar gaay



7- kitni uchai pay hai fikar o kalam ka jalwa

Is ka idrak ali sab ko nahi hosakta

Auj daaim ko ata kar gaay , zainab(as) kay pisar
