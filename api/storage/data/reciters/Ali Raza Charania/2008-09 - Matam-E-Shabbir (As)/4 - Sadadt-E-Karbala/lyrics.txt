Sadadt-e-karbala

Har bibi kar rahi thi kuch iss tarha say girya.

1- kuch bibiya yaha kuch bibiya waha

karti rahi fuga x2

bikhri howi thi maqtal-e-sarwar mai ja baja.

2-ek bibi dastay khooni mai jati hai nahar pay

kahti bachasmay tar x2

abbas(as) deanay aii huu pursa shaheed ka

3-ghut ghut kay ladli teri zinda may mar gaii

turbat wahi bani

bhaiya wo qaid say na howi mar kay bhi riha

4- akbar ki qabar per yu howi laila noha gar

deakha na saal bhar

aii huu qaid sham say ab ho kay mai riha

5- banu yea bain karti thi ay meray bay zuban

maa dhoonday abb kaha

batla day hai kaha mera asghar ay karbala

6- qurban teray ay meray ma jaay bay watan

majboor thi bahain

zindan mai muja say teri aamaanat howi juda

7- rotay hai khas o aam tera sun kay yea kalam

ay shah kay ghulam

noha teray annes hai maqbool e bargah.
